# Command: /patent:claim-draft

Generate draft patent claims for an invention.

## Output Format
```
DRAFT CLAIMS
Invention: [description]

1. [Independent claim — broadest scope]

2. The [element] of claim 1, further comprising [additional feature].

3. The [element] of claim 1, wherein [specific limitation].

[Continue with additional dependent claims]

[Second independent claim — different claim type (method/system)]

NOTES:
- Claim 1 scope: [broad/narrow, strengths, risks]
- Prior art concerns: [specific limitations to consider]
- Recommended narrowing amendments if needed: [suggestions]
```
