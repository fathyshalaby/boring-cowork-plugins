# Patent Portfolio Management
Build and manage a strategic patent portfolio:

### Portfolio Strategy
- **Core patents**: Cover your primary products and their key differentiating features.
- **Design-around blockers**: File patents that cover alternative implementations competitors might use to design around your core patents.
- **Picket fence**: File multiple related patents to create a dense coverage area.
- **Continuation practice**: Use continuations and CIPs to pursue additional claims and adapt to market developments.

### Portfolio Maintenance
| Fee | Due Date |
|-----|----------|
| 3.5-year maintenance fee | 3-3.5 years after grant |
| 7.5-year maintenance fee | 7-7.5 years after grant |
| 11.5-year maintenance fee | 11-11.5 years after grant |

- Failure to pay = patent expires.
- Review portfolio annually: Are maintenance fees justified? Prune patents that no longer align with business strategy.

### Metrics
- **Portfolio size**: Total active patents and pending applications.
- **Coverage map**: Technology areas covered (visualize with patent landscape maps).
- **Citation index**: How often your patents are cited by others (indicates influence).
- **Cost per patent**: Total prosecution cost ÷ Number of patents granted.
- **Revenue attribution**: Licensing revenue, litigation settlements, or competitive advantage attributable to the portfolio.
