# Patent Valuation & Licensing
Value patents and negotiate licensing agreements:

### Valuation Methods
| Method | Approach | Best For |
|--------|---------|----------|
| Cost approach | What did it cost to develop the invention? | Defensive portfolio, internal valuations |
| Market approach | Comparable license transactions | When comparable deals exist |
| Income approach | Projected incremental revenue attributable to the patent | Licensing negotiations, litigation damages |

### Reasonable Royalty Factors (Georgia-Pacific)
Court-established factors for determining reasonable royalty in patent litigation, but also useful for licensing negotiations:
- Existing royalty rates for the patent.
- Rates paid for comparable patents.
- Nature and scope of the license (exclusive vs. non-exclusive).
- Licensor's established policy for licensing.
- Commercial relationship between licensor and licensee.
- Profit of the patented product.
- Portion of profit attributable to the patent vs. unpatented elements.

### License Term Sheet
- **Grant**: Exclusive or non-exclusive, field of use limitations, territory.
- **Royalty**: Running royalty (% of net sales), lump sum, or combination. Minimum annual royalty.
- **Term**: Until patent expiration or a fixed term.
- **Audit rights**: Right to audit licensee's books.
- **Sublicensing**: Permitted or restricted.
- **Enforcement**: Who enforces against third-party infringers?
