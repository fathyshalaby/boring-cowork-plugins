# Command: /pest:prep-sheet

Generate client preparation instructions for an upcoming treatment.

## Output Format
```
PREPARATION INSTRUCTIONS
Service: [type — bed bug, cockroach, general, fumigation]
Scheduled Date: [date] | Time: [time]

BEFORE OUR ARRIVAL:
☐ [Specific preparation step]
☐ [Step 2]
☐ [Step 3]
[etc.]

DURING TREATMENT:
[Where to be, pets, HVAC, etc.]

AFTER TREATMENT:
• [Re-entry time / ventilation instructions]
• [What to expect (increased activity is normal)]
• [When to contact us]

⚠️ SAFETY INFORMATION:
[Product-specific precautions, pet safety, child safety]

QUESTIONS? Call: [phone] | Email: [email]
```
