# Pesticide Application & Safety
Apply pesticides safely, legally, and effectively:

### Label Compliance
- **The label is the law** (FIFRA). Follow all directions: rate, application method, PPE, REI, and precautionary statements.
- **Signal words**: CAUTION (least toxic) → WARNING → DANGER (most toxic). Know what they mean and adjust PPE accordingly.
- **Restricted Use Pesticides (RUP)**: Only certified applicators may purchase and apply RUPs.

### Application Methods
| Method | Equipment | Best For |
|--------|----------|----------|
| Crack & crevice | Injection gun, straw tip | Cockroaches, ants in structural voids |
| Spot treatment | Compressed sprayer | Targeted areas <2 sq ft |
| Bait placement | Bait gun, stations | Cockroaches, ants, rodents |
| Dust application | Hand duster, electric duster | Voids, attics, behind outlets |
| Mist/ULV | ULV fogger | Mosquitoes, stored product pests |
| Granular | Spreader | Exterior perimeter, turf pests |
| Fumigation | Tarp + gas (Vikane) | Drywood termites, stored products |

### Calibration
- Calibrate sprayers before every job. Incorrect calibration = wrong dose = ineffective or illegal application.
- Flow rate (oz/min) × spray time = volume applied. Verify against label rate per unit area.

### PPE Requirements
- Minimum PPE for most applications: chemical-resistant gloves, long sleeves/pants, closed-toe shoes, eye protection.
- Fumigation: SCBA (Self-Contained Breathing Apparatus), certified fumigation technician required.
