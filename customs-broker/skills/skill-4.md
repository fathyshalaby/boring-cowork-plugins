# Trade Compliance & Regulations
Maintain compliance with all trade regulations:

### Key Regulatory Agencies
- **CBP**: Entry, classification, valuation, marking, trade enforcement.
- **FDA**: Food, drugs, cosmetics, medical devices. Prior notice required for food.
- **USDA/APHIS**: Agricultural products, plant/animal health.
- **EPA/TSCA**: Chemicals, pesticides, vehicles (emissions).
- **FCC**: Electronics, telecommunications equipment.
- **CPSC**: Consumer products safety.
- **TTB**: Alcohol, tobacco.
- **OFAC**: Sanctions, embargoes, denied parties.

### Reasonable Care Standard
- Importers must exercise **reasonable care** in classifying, valuing, and entering goods. Failure = penalties.
- Document your compliance efforts: classification rulings, valuation records, origin certifications.
- Conduct periodic internal audits.

### Penalties
| Violation | Penalty |
|-----------|--------|
| Negligence | 2x loss of revenue (or 20% of dutiable value) |
| Gross negligence | 4x loss of revenue (or 40% of dutiable value) |
| Fraud | Full domestic value of merchandise |
| ISF late filing | $5,000 per violation |
| Marking violation | 10% of value + relabeling costs |
