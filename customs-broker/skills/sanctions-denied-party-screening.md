# Sanctions & Denied Party Screening
Screen all transactions against trade sanctions and denied party lists:

### OFAC Sanctions Programs
- Comprehensive sanctions: Cuba, Iran, North Korea, Syria, Crimea region.
- Sectoral sanctions: Russia, Venezuela (specific sectors).
- SDN List: Specially Designated Nationals—individuals and entities blocked from all transactions with US persons.

### Screening Requirements
- Screen ALL parties to every transaction: buyer, seller, consignee, freight forwarder, end user, financial institutions.
- Screen against: OFAC SDN List, Entity List (BIS), Denied Persons List (BIS), Unverified List (BIS), Debarred List (DDTC), EU Consolidated List.
- Screen at multiple transaction touchpoints: booking, entry filing, and payment.

### Red Flags
- Customer reluctant to provide end-use or end-user information.
- Shipment routed through unusual or sanctioned countries.
- Products ordered inconsistent with the buyer's stated business.
- Payment from a third party in an unrelated country.
- Customer willing to pay cash for expensive items normally financed.
