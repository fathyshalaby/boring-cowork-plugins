# Import/Export Documentation
Manage trade documentation accurately:

### Commercial Invoice Requirements
Must contain: seller/buyer names and addresses, date, terms of sale (Incoterms), description, HTS number, quantity, unit value, total value, country of origin, currency.

### Bills of Lading
- **Ocean B/L**: Negotiable (original required for release) or non-negotiable (telex/sea waybill).
- **Air Waybill**: Always non-negotiable. Serves as receipt and contract of carriage.
- **Multimodal B/L**: Covers multiple modes of transport.

### Certificates
| Certificate | When Required |
|-------------|---------------|
| Certificate of Origin | FTA claims, certain country requirements |
| Phytosanitary Certificate | Plant products (USDA APHIS) |
| Health Certificate | Animal products, food items |
| Fumigation Certificate | Wood packaging (ISPM 15) |
| Conformity Certificate | Certain consumer goods, electronics |
| Textile Declaration | Textile and apparel products |
