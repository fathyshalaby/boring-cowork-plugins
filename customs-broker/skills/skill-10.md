# C-TPAT & Trusted Trader Programs
Leverage trusted trader programs for supply chain security and expedited clearance:

### C-TPAT (Customs-Trade Partnership Against Terrorism)
- Voluntary public-private partnership where importers implement supply chain security best practices.
- **Benefits**: Reduced examination rates (4-6x fewer exams), front of the line for exams, eligibility for FAST lanes at land borders, access to the CTPAT Portal.
- **Tiers**: Tier 2 (certified) → Tier 3 (best practices partner, additional benefits).
- **Requirements**: Written security profile, supply chain security procedures, foreign manufacturer/supplier verification.

### AEO Mutual Recognition
- C-TPAT has mutual recognition agreements with multiple countries' Authorized Economic Operator (AEO) programs.
- Benefits extend to partner countries: faster clearance in EU, Japan, South Korea, and other MRA partners.

### Maintaining Certification
- Annual self-assessment of security practices.
- CBP validation visit every 3-5 years.
- Report security incidents and breaches promptly.
- Monitor and verify all supply chain partners on an ongoing basis.
