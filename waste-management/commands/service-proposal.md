# Command: /waste:service-proposal

Generate a professional waste management service proposal for a prospective commercial client.

## Output Format
```
WASTE MANAGEMENT SERVICE PROPOSAL
Prepared For: [Client Name]
Prepared By: [Company] | Date: [date]

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY:
[1-2 paragraphs summarizing the proposed service and value proposition]

PROPOSED SERVICES:
Service          Container    Frequency    Monthly Rate
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Solid Waste      [X]-yard     [X]x/week    $[X]
Recycling        [X]-yard     [X]x/week    $[X]
Organics         [X]-yard     [X]x/week    $[X]

TOTAL MONTHLY COST: $[X]
CONTRACT TERM: [X] years

INCLUDED:
• Container delivery and exchange at no charge
• Quarterly waste stream consultation
• Annual sustainability report
• 24/7 emergency service line

VALUE-ADDED:
• Estimated diversion rate improvement: [X]%
• Estimated annual cost savings vs. current provider: $[X]
• Sustainability reporting for ESG/LEED compliance

TERMS & CONDITIONS:
[Standard contract terms summary]
```
