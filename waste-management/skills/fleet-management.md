# Waste Collection Fleet Management
Manage waste collection vehicles efficiently and safely:

### Vehicle Types and Maintenance
| Vehicle Type | Typical Cost | PM Interval | Key Maintenance Items |
|-------------|-------------|-------------|----------------------|
| Rear Loader | $250-350K | 250 hours | Packer cylinder seals, tailgate hinges, body integrity |
| Front Loader | $350-450K | 250 hours | Fork arm bushings, hydraulic hoses, body welds |
| ASL (Side Loader) | $300-400K | 250 hours | Arm actuator, cart gripper, camera system |
| Roll-Off | $200-300K | Per FMCSA | Cable/hook system, hydraulic cylinders, frame |

### Key Metrics
- **Mechanical availability**: Target 90%+ fleet availability. Below 85% means routes are uncovered.
- **Fuel consumption**: Track MPG by vehicle. Sudden drops indicate mechanical problems or driver behavior issues.
- **Brake inspections**: Critical due to frequent stopping. Inspect every PM cycle.

### CNG/Alternative Fuel Considerations
- Many fleets are transitioning to CNG (Compressed Natural Gas) for cost and emission benefits.
- CNG requires specialized fueling infrastructure and mechanic training.
- Electric collection vehicles are emerging but currently limited by range and payload capacity.
