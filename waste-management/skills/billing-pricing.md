# Billing, Pricing & Revenue
Structure waste management billing and pricing correctly:

### Pricing Models
- **Residential**: Monthly flat rate per household. Varies by service level (cart size, recycling inclusion).
- **Commercial**: Based on container size × pickup frequency × haul distance. Typical: $X per lift.
- **Roll-off**: Per-haul fee + disposal (per-ton tipping fee) + daily rental after X free days.
- **Special waste**: Pricing based on waste characterization, volume, and disposal destination. Always get a waste profile before quoting.

### Common Fee Components
| Fee | Description | Typical Amount |
|-----|------------|---------------|
| Base service fee | Container rental + collection | Varies by size/frequency |
| Disposal/tipping fee | Per-ton landfill charge | $40-80/ton (varies widely) |
| Fuel/environmental surcharge | Fluctuates with diesel prices | 8-15% of base |
| Contamination fee | Recyclables with >X% contamination | $50-250 per occurrence |
| Overage/extra pickup | Excess waste or unscheduled service | $25-100 per event |
| Lock/unlock fee | For gated or restricted access sites | $15-50 per occurrence |

### Revenue Protection
- Review accounts annually for rate increases (CPI-based, min 3%).
- Audit weight tickets against billed amounts monthly.
- Monitor accounts for unauthorized use (non-customer dumping in commercial containers).
