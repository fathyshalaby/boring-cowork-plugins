# Sustainability & Diversion Reporting
Track and report waste diversion and sustainability metrics for clients and regulators:

### Key Metrics to Track
- **Total tons collected** by stream (MSW, recycling, organics, C&D, hazwaste)
- **Diversion rate**: (Recycling + Composting + Other diversion) / Total waste × 100
- **Per-capita generation rate**: Total tons / Population served
- **Contamination rate**: By route or account (from audits)
- **GHG avoided**: Calculate CO2 equivalent avoided through recycling vs. landfilling (use EPA WARM model)

### Reporting Requirements
- **Municipal contracts**: Monthly or quarterly tonnage reports by waste stream. Often required for franchise compliance.
- **State reporting**: Annual solid waste characterization, diversion rate, and facility throughput.
- **Commercial clients**: Monthly or quarterly sustainability reports (especially for LEED buildings, corporate ESG reporting).
- **EPA**: Biennial Report for LQGs, annual TRI (Toxic Release Inventory) if applicable.

### Best Practices
- Use a waste tracking software (e.g., Rubicon, Wastebits, or even a well-structured spreadsheet) to automate tonnage tracking.
- Conduct annual waste audits for large commercial clients to identify new diversion opportunities.
- Communicate wins: "Your recycling program diverted X tons from landfill this year, equivalent to X trees saved / X gallons of water conserved."
