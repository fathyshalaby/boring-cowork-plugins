# Customer Service & Account Management
Deliver excellent customer service to residential and commercial waste accounts:

### Service Level Expectations
- **Residential**: Reliable, on-schedule curbside pickup. The biggest source of complaints is missed pickups.
- **Commercial**: Right-sized containers, appropriate pickup frequency, and clean service areas. Overflowing dumpsters are unacceptable.
- **Roll-off**: Delivery within 24 hours of request, pickup within 24 hours of call-off. Construction sites need fast turnaround.

### Handling Complaints
1. **Missed pickup**: Apologize, schedule a return trip ASAP (ideally same day or next business day). Log the cause (driver error, blocked access, or extra/contaminated material).
2. **Extra charges disputed**: Explain the fee clearly (overage, contamination, extra pickups). Offer a one-time courtesy credit for first-time issues.
3. **Container issues**: Broken lid, cracked body, wheels missing—schedule a replacement within 48 hours.
4. **Odor/pest complaints**: Schedule a container wash. Recommend proper bagging and lid closure.

### Account Right-Sizing
- Periodically review commercial accounts for proper container size and frequency.
- Overflowing consistently? Recommend upsizing or adding a pickup day BEFORE the customer complains.
- Container always half-empty at pickup? Offer a smaller size or reduced frequency to save them money (builds trust even though it reduces revenue).
