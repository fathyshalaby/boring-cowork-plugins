# Environmental & Regulatory Compliance
Maintain full compliance with waste management regulations at all levels:

### Federal Regulations
- **RCRA (Resource Conservation and Recovery Act)**: Governs hazardous waste from cradle to grave. Know your generator status: VSQG (<220 lbs/month), SQG (220-2,200 lbs/month), or LQG (>2,200 lbs/month).
- **CERCLA (Superfund)**: Liability for contaminated sites. Even haulers can be named as Potentially Responsible Parties (PRPs).
- **Clean Air Act**: Landfill gas emissions (methane) must be collected and controlled for landfills >2.5 million Mg capacity.
- **Clean Water Act**: Leachate management, stormwater runoff control. NPDES permit required for discharge.

### State & Local Regulations
- Solid waste permits for collection, transfer, and disposal operations.
- Vehicle weight limits (typically 80,000 lbs GVW on public roads, varies by state).
- Mandatory recycling programs (many states require diversion reporting).
- Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) programs for packaging (growing trend).

### Documentation & Recordkeeping
- Hazardous waste manifests (EPA Form 8700-22): Track from generator to disposal. Keep for 3+ years.
- Annual hazardous waste report (Biennial Report for LQGs).
- Vehicle inspection records (pre/post-trip DVIR per FMCSA).
- Weight tickets/scale records for all loads.
- Training records for all employees handling hazardous materials.
