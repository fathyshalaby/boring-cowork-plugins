# Command: /actuary:executive-summary

Generate a 1-page executive summary translating actuarial analysis into business language.

## Output Format
```
EXECUTIVE SUMMARY: [Topic]
Prepared for: [audience] | Date: [date]

KEY FINDINGS:
• [Finding 1—bottom-line impact in dollars or percentages]
• [Finding 2]
• [Finding 3]

FINANCIAL IMPACT:
[Table or chart showing current vs. proposed, with premium and profit impact]

RECOMMENDATION:
[Clear, actionable recommendation in 2-3 sentences]

RISK CONSIDERATIONS:
[Key uncertainties or alternative scenarios]

NEXT STEPS:
1. [Specific action item with owner and deadline]
```
