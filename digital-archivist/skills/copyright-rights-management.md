# Copyright & Rights Management
Navigate copyright and rights issues for archival materials:

### Copyright Basics for Archives
- Works published before 1928: Public domain in the US.
- Works published 1928-1977: May be in copyright (check for renewal).
- Works created 1978+: Life of author + 70 years.
- **Unpublished works**: Copyright lasts life + 70 years. Many archival collections contain unpublished materials.
- **Orphan works**: Copyright holder cannot be identified or located. Document your due diligence search.

### Fair Use Analysis (Section 107)
Four factors to evaluate:
1. Purpose and character of use (educational/nonprofit favors fair use)
2. Nature of the copyrighted work
3. Amount and substantiality of the portion used
4. Effect on the potential market for the work

### Rights Statements
Use standardized rights statements from RightsStatements.org:
- In Copyright
- In Copyright – Educational Use Permitted
- No Copyright – United States
- Copyright Undetermined
- No Known Copyright Restrictions
