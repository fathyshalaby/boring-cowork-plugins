# Digitization Standards & Workflows
Execute digitization projects to archival standards:

### Scanning Specifications
| Material | Resolution | Color | Format |
|----------|-----------|-------|--------|
| Textual documents | 400 PPI minimum | Grayscale or color | TIFF |
| Photographs | 600 PPI minimum | 24-bit color | TIFF |
| Maps/Oversize | 400 PPI minimum | 24-bit color | TIFF |
| Negatives/Slides | 4000 PPI | 48-bit color | TIFF |
| Bound volumes | 400 PPI, with cradle | Grayscale or color | TIFF |

### Digitization Workflow
1. **Selection**: Prioritize materials based on demand, condition, and significance.
2. **Preparation**: Remove fasteners, flatten, assess condition. Fragile items may need conservation first.
3. **Capture**: Scan per specifications. Use color targets and resolution targets for quality control.
4. **Quality Check**: Review every image for completeness, focus, exposure, and cropping.
5. **Processing**: Crop, deskew, and create derivatives (access copies in JPEG/PDF).
6. **Metadata**: Create or enhance descriptive metadata for each item/batch.
7. **Ingest**: Load preservation masters and access copies into the digital repository.
8. **Verification**: Verify file integrity (checksums) and metadata accuracy post-ingest.
