# Command: /eu-grants:impact-pathway

Map project results to EU expected impacts (Section 2.1 of proposal).

## Output Format
```
PATHWAY TO IMPACT STATEMENT

1. PROJECT RESULTS (Outputs)
What is generated during the project?
• [e.g., A novel algorithm for X; A prototype of Y; A policy whitepaper]

2. DISSEMINATION/EXPLOITATION CHANNELS
How are results pushed to target groups?
• [e.g., Open access journals, trade fairs, patent filing, open source repo]

3. TARGET GROUPS
Who will use the results?
• [e.g., Component manufacturers, EU regulators, citizen science groups]

4. OUTCOMES (Short/Medium Term - Just after project)
What changes do the target groups make because of the results?
• [e.g., Manufacturers adopt the prototype; Regulators update standard X]

5. IMPACTS (Long Term)
How does this address the Destination level impacts in the Work Programme?
• Scientific: [Advance the field of X by Y%]
• Economic/Technological: [Reduce cost to market by Y%; create X new jobs]
• Societal/Environmental: [Reduce CO2 emissions by Y tons; align with Green Deal]
```
