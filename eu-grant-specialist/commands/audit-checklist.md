# Command: /eu-grants:audit-checklist

Generate a preparation checklist for an upcoming ex-post financial audit.

## Output Format
```
EU GRANT AUDIT PREPARATION CHECKLIST
Project Acronym: [Name] | Grant Agreement: [Number]

PERSONNEL COSTS
☐ Signed employment contracts for all claimed staff
☐ Payroll records / payslips proving actual costs incurred
☐ Proof of payment (bank transfers) to employees
☐ Timesheets signed by employee and supervisor OR monthly declarations
☐ Calculation formulas for daily/hourly rates well documented

SUBCONTRACTING & PURCHASES
☐ Evidence of 'best value for money' procedures (3 quotes minimum for large purchases)
☐ Copies of signed contracts/POs with suppliers
☐ Original invoices received
☐ Proof of payment (bank statements)
☐ Proof of delivery of goods/services

TRAVEL
☐ Invoices/receipts for flights, hotels, transit
☐ Boarding passes / train tickets (proof travel actually occurred)
☐ Proof connection to project (meeting agendas, signed attendance lists)

GENERAL
☐ Final version of the Consortium Agreement
☐ Extract from general ledger showing project accounting codes used
☐ No deductible VAT claimed as eligible costs
☐ Exchange rate calculations (if non-Euro country) follow GA rules
```
