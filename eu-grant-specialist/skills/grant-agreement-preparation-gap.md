# Grant Agreement Preparation (GAP)
Manage the phase between proposal success and project start:

### The GAP Timeline
- Max 3 months from evaluation results to signature (Strict timetable).
- **Invitation to prepare grant**: The coordinator receives the Evaluation Summary Report (ESR) and invitation.
- **Data updates**: Ensure LEAR, FSIGN, and LSIGN are appointed for all partners. Validate PICs (legal status and financial capacity check by EC Validation Services).

### Key GAP Steps
1. **Description of the Action (DoA - Annex 1)**: Sync the evaluated proposal into the structured IT system. The EC will ask you to resolve any minor issues raised by evaluators (but you cannot make substantial changes to the work or budget).
2. **Financial Capacity Check**: EC checks if the coordinator (and sometimes other partners) has the financial health to implement the project. May result in requirements for pre-financing guarantees or trust accounts.
3. **Ethics / Security Review**: Fulfill any requirements resulting from the pre-award ethics or security screenings (e.g., appointing an independent ethics advisor).
4. **Signatures**: Done entirely online. Coordinator's LSIGN signs first, then the EU Project Officer. 
5. **Accession**: Within 30 days of the GA entry into force, all other beneficiaries must sign the Accession Form in the portal.

### Pre-financing
- Disbursed to the coordinator shortly after signature (usually ~30 days, or 10 days before the start date).
- A portion (usually 5%) is immediately retained for the Mutual Insurance Mechanism (formerly Guarantee Fund).
