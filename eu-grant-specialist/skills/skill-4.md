# Consortium Building & Management
Form and manage effective transnational partnerships:

### Minimum Eligibility Conditions (Standard)
For most Horizon collaborative projects (RIA/IA):
- At least 3 independent legal entities.
- Established in 3 different EU Member States (MS) or Associated Countries (AC).
- At least 1 must be established in an MS.

### Partner Roles
| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| Coordinator | Legal/administrative interface with the EC, project management, distributes funds. |
| Beneficiary | Full partner, receives EC funding, signs Grant Agreement. |
| Affiliated Entity | Linked to a beneficiary (e.g., subsidiary, joint venture). Does work and claims costs via the beneficiary. |
| Associated Partner | Participates in the action but does NOT receive EU funding (often from non-associated third countries like USA, or UK/Swiss depending on current association status). |
| Subcontractor | Provides specific services at market price, profit margin included. Does not sign the agreement. |

### The Consortium Agreement (CA)
- Mandatory internal agreement between beneficiaries (EC is not a party).
- Must be signed *before* signing the Grant Agreement with the EC.
- Covers: Internal organization, voting rules, distribution of funds, liability, and Intellectual Property Rights (background IP, ownership of foreground IP, access rights). DESCA is the standard template used for Horizon.
