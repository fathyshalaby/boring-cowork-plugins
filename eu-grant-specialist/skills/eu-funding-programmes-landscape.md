# EU Funding Programmes Landscape
Navigate the Multiannual Financial Framework (MFF) 2021-2027:

### Major Funding Programmes
| Programme | Focus Area | Budget (Approx) | Management |
|-----------|------------|-----------------|------------|
| Horizon Europe | R&I, Science, Global Challenges | €95.5 billion | Direct (EC/Agencies) |
| ERDF (Cohesion) | Regional development, smart growth | €200+ billion | Shared (Member States) |
| ESF+ (Cohesion) | Employment, education, social inclusion | €88 billion | Shared (Member States) |
| Digital Europe | AI, cybersecurity, supercomputing | €7.5 billion | Direct |
| LIFE | Environment, climate action | €5.4 billion | Direct (CINEA) |
| Erasmus+ | Education, training, youth, sport | €26.2 billion | Direct & Indirect (National Agencies) |
| CEF (Connecting EU) | Transport, energy, digital networks | €33.7 billion | Direct (CINEA) |
| EU4Health | Health systems, crisis preparedness | €5.1 billion | Direct (HaDEA) |

### Management Modes
1. **Direct Management**: Managed directly by the European Commission or Executive Agencies (e.g., REA, CINEA, HaDEA). You apply directly via the EU Funding & Tenders Portal.
2. **Shared Management**: Administered by Member State authorities (Managing Authorities). Example: Regional structural funds. You apply through national/regional portals, following local interpretation of EU rules.
3. **Indirect Management**: Handed over to partner organisations or other authorities (e.g., EIB, national agencies).
