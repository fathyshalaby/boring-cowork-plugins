# Project Reporting & Reviews
Manage technical and financial reporting to ensure seamless payments:

### Reporting Periods (RPs)
Typical 36-month project has two RPs (M1-M18, M19-M36). Within **60 days** following the end of an RP, the consortium must submit periodic reports.

### 1. Technical Report
- **Part A (Data)**: Generated via web forms based on continuous reporting (Deliverables, Milestones, Publications, Patents, Gender data, Dissemination activities).
- **Part B (Narrative)**: Uploaded PDF explaining the work carried out, progress towards objectives, deviations from Annex 1, and impact.

### 2. Financial Report
- Individual Financial Statement (Annex 4) submitted by each beneficiary in the portal.
- **Certificate on the Financial Statements (CFS)**: An audit report produced by an independent auditor. Required ONLY at the end of the project for beneficiaries declaring ≥ €430,000 in actual costs (excludes lump sums and flat rates).
- The coordinator consolidates and submits the request for payment.

### Interim Payments
- EC has 90 days to evaluate the reports and issue payment.
- Pre-financing + Interim payments are capped at 90% of the total grant amount. The final 10% (and release of the Mutual Insurance Mechanism contribution) happens at the final payment.

### EC Reviews
The Project Officer (PO) may organize technical reviews with external monitors. Preparation is key: present clear progress, be transparent about delays, and propose mitigation strategies.
