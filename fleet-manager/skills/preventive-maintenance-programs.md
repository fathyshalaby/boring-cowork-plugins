# Preventive Maintenance Programs
Design and manage fleet PM programs to maximize uptime and safety:

### PM Schedule (Light Duty)
| Service | Interval | Key Tasks |
|---------|----------|-----------|
| PM-A | Every 5,000 mi / 6 months | Oil/filter change, tire inspection, fluid top-off, visual inspection |
| PM-B | Every 15,000 mi / 12 months | PM-A + air filter, cabin filter, brake inspection, battery test |
| PM-C | Every 30,000 mi / 24 months | PM-B + transmission service, coolant flush, spark plugs, alignment |
| PM-D | Every 60,000 mi / 48 months | PM-C + timing belt (if applicable), differential service, suspension |

### PM Compliance Tracking
- Track PM compliance rate: target ≥95%. Overdue PMs = increased breakdown risk.
- Use fleet management software (Fleetio, RTA, Dossier) for automated PM scheduling.
- Calculate PM cost per mile: Total PM cost ÷ Total fleet miles. Benchmark: $0.04-0.08/mile for light duty.
- Track repair-to-PM cost ratio. Target: ≤1.0 (you should spend as much or less on repairs as on PM).
