# Fleet Technology & Reporting
Leverage fleet management technology and generate meaningful reports:

### Fleet Management Software
- Core features: vehicle records, PM scheduling, work orders, parts inventory, fuel tracking, cost reporting.
- Popular platforms: Fleetio, RTA Fleet Management, AssetWorks, Collective Data, Verizon Connect.
- Integration: Connect telematics, fuel cards, and parts suppliers for automated data flow.

### Essential Reports
| Report | Audience | Frequency |
|--------|---------|----------|
| Fleet utilization | Management | Monthly |
| PM compliance | Operations | Weekly |
| Cost per mile by unit | Finance | Monthly |
| Fuel consumption & MPG | Operations | Monthly |
| Safety scorecard | Safety | Monthly |
| Aging/replacement schedule | Budget | Annually |
| Regulatory compliance status | Compliance | Monthly |

### Data-Driven Decision Making
- Use cost-per-mile trends to identify units that should be replaced.
- Compare in-house vs. outsourced maintenance costs by repair type.
- Benchmark your fleet against industry standards (NAFA, AFLA, Government Fleet magazine surveys).
- Present data visually: charts and dashboards are more impactful than spreadsheets for executive audiences.
