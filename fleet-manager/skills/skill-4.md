# Fuel Management & Cost Control
Optimize fuel consumption and control fleet fuel costs:

### Fuel Cost Reduction Strategies
1. **Right-sizing**: Match vehicle size to the job. Don't put a 1-ton truck on a job requiring a half-ton.
2. **Route optimization**: GPS routing to minimize miles driven. 10% fewer miles = 10% less fuel.
3. **Driver behavior**: Aggressive driving (speeding, hard braking, rapid acceleration) increases fuel consumption 15-30%. Use telematics to coach.
4. **Idle reduction**: Idling burns 0.5-1.0 gallons/hour. Anti-idle policies + telematics alerts.
5. **Tire pressure**: Under-inflated tires increase fuel consumption 0.2% per PSI below optimal. Check monthly.
6. **PM compliance**: Well-maintained vehicles operate more efficiently. Dirty air filters alone can reduce MPG 10%.

### Fuel Card Management
- Use fleet fuel cards (WEX, Voyager, Comdata) for centralized purchasing and reporting.
- Set transaction limits: gallons per fill, daily purchase limit, fuel-only restrictions.
- Monitor for fraud: purchases on weekends/off-hours, excessive gallons for vehicle tank size, purchases at non-fuel merchants.
- Negotiate fuel discounts: Fleet fuel programs typically save $0.03-$0.10 per gallon.
