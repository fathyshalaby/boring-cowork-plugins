# Fleet Budgeting & Financial Analysis
Develop and manage fleet budgets:

### Budget Categories
| Category | Typical % of Fleet Cost |
|----------|------------------------|
| Depreciation/Lease payments | 30-40% |
| Fuel | 20-30% |
| Maintenance & repair | 15-20% |
| Insurance | 5-10% |
| Registration, taxes, fees | 2-5% |
| Telematics & technology | 1-3% |

### Key Financial Metrics
- **Cost per mile (CPM)**: Total fleet cost ÷ Total miles. Industry benchmarks: Light duty $0.50-$0.75/mi, Medium duty $0.60-$0.90/mi, Heavy duty $0.80-$1.50/mi.
- **Cost per unit per month**: Total cost ÷ (Units × Months). Useful for comparing to lease rates.
- **Availability rate**: (Available units ÷ Total units) × 100%. Target: ≥95%.
- **Utilization rate**: (Units actively used ÷ Total units) × 100%. Low utilization (<60%) suggests oversized fleet.

### Replacement Planning
- Lifecycle cost analysis: When does the cost of keeping a vehicle exceed the cost of replacing it?
- Replacement criteria: age (years), mileage, maintenance cost threshold (when annual repair cost exceeds 50% of vehicle value), condition score, reliability (breakdown frequency).
