# Fleet Operations & Vehicle Lifecycle
Manage the complete vehicle lifecycle from acquisition to disposal:

### Vehicle Lifecycle Phases
| Phase | Key Activities | Timeline |
|-------|---------------|----------|
| Specification | Define requirements (payload, fuel type, body config), develop bid specs | 3-6 months before acquisition |
| Acquisition | Competitive bidding, purchase/lease analysis, ordering | 2-6 months lead time |
| Commissioning | Upfitting (lights, racks, graphics), driver assignment, telematics install | 2-4 weeks |
| Operations | Daily operations, preventive maintenance, fuel management | 5-15 years typical |
| Replacement analysis | TCO review, condition assessment, resale timing | Annual review |
| Disposal | Auction, trade-in, or direct sale. De-commission (remove graphics, equipment) | 2-4 weeks |

### Buy vs. Lease Analysis
- **Purchase**: Lower long-term cost, full control, capital investment, depreciation tax benefit.
- **Lease**: Lower upfront cost, predictable monthly cost, off-balance-sheet (operating lease), easier fleet rotation.
- **Decision factors**: Annual mileage, holding period, cash flow, tax situation, maintenance capability.
- Calculate Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) for both scenarios over the expected holding period.
