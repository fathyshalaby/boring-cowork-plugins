# DOT Compliance & Regulations
Maintain compliance with DOT and FMCSA regulations:

### FMCSA Regulations for Commercial Vehicles (>10,001 lbs GVWR)
- **CDL requirements**: Class A (>26,001 lbs combination), Class B (>26,001 lbs single), Class C (hazmat, 16+ passengers).
- **Hours of Service (HOS)**: 11-hour driving limit, 14-hour on-duty limit, 10 consecutive hours off-duty. ELD mandate for HOS recording.
- **Driver Qualification File (DQF)**: Application, MVR (annual), medical certificate (DOT physical every 2 years), road test certificate, previous employer inquiries.
- **Annual Vehicle Inspections**: FMCSA requires annual inspection by qualified inspector. Inspection report kept on vehicle or at principal place of business.
- **Pre/Post-Trip Inspections**: Drivers must conduct daily pre-trip and post-trip inspections per 49 CFR 396.13. Document defects on DVIR (Driver Vehicle Inspection Report).

### IFTA (International Fuel Tax Agreement)
- Required for vehicles >26,000 lbs GVWR or 3+ axles operating in 2+ jurisdictions.
- File quarterly. Report miles driven and fuel purchased by jurisdiction.
- Retain fuel receipts for 4 years.
