# Fleet Safety & Accident Management
Build a fleet safety program and manage accidents effectively:

### Accident Response Protocol
1. Ensure safety of all persons. Call 911 if injuries.
2. Secure the scene (triangles/flares if safe).
3. Exchange information with other parties.
4. Document: photos of all vehicles, damage, scene, road conditions, traffic signs.
5. File a police report.
6. Notify fleet manager/dispatcher immediately.
7. Complete internal accident report within 24 hours.
8. Drug and alcohol post-accident testing (DOT-regulated drivers: required if fatality, citation + injury, or citation + tow-away).

### Preventable Accident Determination
- Use the NSC (National Safety Council) definition: "A preventable accident is one in which the driver failed to do everything reasonably possible to avoid it."
- Convene an accident review committee for all incidents.
- Track preventable accident rate: target <1 per million miles.

### Driver Training
- Defensive driving training at hire and annually.
- Smith System or similar methodology: Aim high in steering, get the big picture, keep your eyes moving, leave yourself an out, make sure they see you.
- Specialized training: backing (largest single cause of fleet accidents), winter driving, towing.
