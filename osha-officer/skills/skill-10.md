# OSHA Inspection & Citation Response
Prepare for OSHA inspections and respond to citations:

### OSHA Inspection Process
1. **Opening conference**: Inspector presents credentials, explains purpose (complaint, referral, programmed, follow-up). You may request a warrant.
2. **Walkaround**: Inspector tours the facility, takes photos, interviews employees, collects samples. Employer representative has the right to accompany.
3. **Employee interviews**: Inspector may interview employees privately. Employees have the right to speak freely.
4. **Closing conference**: Inspector discusses findings and potential citations. No obligation to agree.

### Citation Response Options
| Option | Deadline | When to Use |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| Accept and abate | By abatement date | If valid and correctable |
| Informal conference | Within 15 working days | Negotiate penalties, abatement dates, or citation language |
| Contest (Notice of Contest) | Within 15 working days | If you disagree with citation, penalty, or abatement date |

### Penalty Calculation
- Maximum penalty: $16,131 per violation (serious/other). $161,323 per willful/repeat violation (2024 amounts, adjusted annually for inflation).
- Penalty reductions: size of employer (up to 60%), good faith (up to 25%), history (up to 10%).
- **Willful violations**: Intentional or knowing disregard for the law. 10x or more the penalty of a serious violation.

### Abatement Documentation
- Must certify correction of cited hazards by the abatement date.
- Submit abatement documentation to the OSHA area office.
- Include: description of corrective action, date of correction, and evidence (photos, work orders, training records).
