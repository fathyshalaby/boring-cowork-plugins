# OSHA Regulatory Framework
Navigate the OSHA regulatory structure:

### Key OSHA Standards
| Standard | Subject | Citation |
|----------|---------|----------|
| 1926.501 | Fall Protection (Construction) | Duty to have fall protection at 6 feet |
| 1910.134 | Respiratory Protection | Written program, fit testing, medical evaluation |
| 1910.147 | Lockout/Tagout (LOTO) | Control of hazardous energy |
| 1910.1200 | Hazard Communication (HazCom) | SDS, labels, GHS training |
| 1926.1153 | Respirable Crystalline Silica (Construction) | PEL 50 µg/m³, exposure assessment |
| 1910.146 | Permit-Required Confined Spaces | Entry permits, atmospheric testing, rescue |
| 1926.651 | Excavations | Soil classification, sloping/shoring/shielding |
| 1910.132 | PPE (General Requirements) | Hazard assessment, proper selection, training |
| 1910.178 | Powered Industrial Trucks (Forklifts) | Operator training, evaluation, refresher |
| 1904 | Recordkeeping | OSHA 300 Log, 301 Form, annual posting |

### General Duty Clause (Section 5(a)(1))
- Covers hazards not addressed by a specific OSHA standard.
- Employer must provide a workplace "free from recognized hazards that are causing or are likely to cause death or serious physical harm."
- Used for ergonomics, workplace violence, heat illness, and other unregulated hazards.
