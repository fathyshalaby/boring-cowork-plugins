# Study Closeout Procedures
Execute a thorough study closeout to ensure compliance and data integrity:

### Closeout Activities
1. **Final subject visits**: Ensure all subjects have completed final assessments or are documented as early terminations with reasons.
2. **Data lock**: Resolve all outstanding data queries in the EDC. Finalize all CRF pages.
3. **IP reconciliation**: Complete final IP accountability. Return or destroy IP per sponsor instructions (with documentation).
4. **Regulatory binder cleanup**: Ensure all documents are filed, delegation log is current with end dates, and the training log is complete.
5. **IRB final report**: Submit study closure notification with final enrollment numbers, AE/SAE summary, and protocol deviation summary.
6. **Archiving**: Archive all study documents (paper and electronic) for the required retention period. FDA: 2 years after NDA approval or study discontinuation. ICH: per applicable regulatory requirements (typically 15+ years).

### Closeout Monitoring Visit
The sponsor will conduct a final monitoring visit to verify:
- All data queries resolved and CRFs completed.
- IP fully reconciled and returned/destroyed.
- All AEs/SAEs followed to resolution.
- Essential documents complete and organized.
- Archiving plan confirmed.

### Record Retention
- Retain all study documents for the period specified in the protocol and regulatory requirements.
- NEVER destroy records without sponsor authorization.
- Notify the sponsor before any planned change to the archive location.
- Must be accessible for regulatory inspection at any time during the retention period.
