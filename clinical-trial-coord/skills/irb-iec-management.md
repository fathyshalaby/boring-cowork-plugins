# IRB/IEC Management
Navigate Institutional Review Board/Ethics Committee submissions and ongoing oversight:

### IRB Submission Types
1. **Initial Review**: Submit the complete study package: protocol, ICF, IB, advertisements, recruitment materials, PI CV, and site-specific documents.
2. **Continuing Review/Annual Renewal**: Submit annually with enrollment status, AE/SAE summaries, protocol deviations, and amendments since last review.
3. **Amendments**: Submit all protocol amendments, ICF revisions, and changes to study procedures, personnel, or documents.
4. **Reportable Events**: Report unanticipated problems, new safety information, and protocol deviations per IRB requirements.
5. **Study Closure**: Submit final report with enrollment totals, AE summary, and disposition of subjects.

### Working With Your IRB
- Know your IRB's submission deadlines, meeting schedules, and turnaround times.
- Maintain a regulatory binder organized per FDA 1572 requirements.
- Track approval expiration dates. **Never conduct study activities with an expired IRB approval.**
- Central IRB vs. local IRB: Many multi-site trials use a central IRB (CIRB). Know whether your site uses central or local review.
