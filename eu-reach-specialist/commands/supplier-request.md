# Command: /reach:supplier-request

Draft a formal request to a supplier for REACH and SCIP compliance data.

## Output Format
```
Subject: URGENT: REACH SVHC & SCIP Compliance Declaration for [Company Name]

Dear Compliance Team at [Supplier Company],

To fulfill our legal obligations under the EU REACH Regulation (EC 1907/2006) and the Waste Framework Directive (SCIP Database), [Your Company] requires an updated compliance declaration for all parts supplied to us.

Please review the attached list of parts and provide a declaration confirming:

1. REACH SVHC STATUS: Does the article contain any Substances of Very High Concern (SVHCs) from the current Candidate List ([Number of SVHCs] substances, updated [Date]) in a concentration above 0.1% weight by weight (w/w) at the lowest component level (O5A ruling)?

2. IF YES (SVHC >0.1% present), you MUST provide:
   a) Substance Name and CAS/EC Number.
   b) The specific component/article containing the substance (e.g., "brass pin").
   c) The concentration range of the substance.
   d) Safe use information.

Please provide this data using the attached IPC-1752A XML template or standard IEC 62474 format. Alternatively, complete the attached Excel spreadsheet. 

General statements saying "our products are REACH compliant" without SVHC details are not legally sufficient for our SCIP reporting requirements and will be rejected.

Please respond by [Date]. Failure to provide this data may result in part blocks for EU-destined products.

Sincerely,
[Your Name/Title]
```
