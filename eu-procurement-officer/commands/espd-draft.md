# Command: /eu-proc:espd-draft

Outline the standard ESPD selection criteria parts targeted for a specific tender.

## Output Format
```
ESPD SELECTION CRITERIA OUTLINE
Contract: [Name / Reference]

PART II: INFORMATION CONCERNING THE ECONOMIC OPERATOR
- Base info, reliance on capacities of other entities, use of subcontractors.

PART III: EXCLUSION GROUNDS
- Section A: Grounds relating to criminal convictions (Mandatory exclusion).
- Section B: Payment of taxes and social security (Mandatory exclusion).
- Section C: Grounds relating to insolvency, conflicts of interests or professional misconduct (Discretionary exclusions applied by Authority).

PART IV: SELECTION CRITERIA (Specifically requiring Yes/No or data input)

alpha. Global indication: [Select YES/NO - Used if you just want a single "I meet all criteria" tick box, checking evidence only for winner.]

A: Suitability
- Enrolment in a relevant professional register: [Specify required register, e.g., Chamber of Commerce].

B: Economic and Financial Standing
- Yearly turnover: Minimum required [€ Amount] for the last [3] financial years.
- Professional risk indemnity insurance: Minimum coverage [€ Amount].

C: Technical and Professional Ability
- For service contracts: List principal services delivered over the past 3 years containing sum, dates, and recipients.
- Educational and professional qualifications: [e.g., Minimum 5 years experience for Project Manager role].
```
