# Command: /eu-proc:alt-inquiry-letter

Draft a formal request for explanation regarding an Abnormally Low Tender.

## Output Format
```
REQUEST FOR EXPLANATION: POTENTIAL ABNORMALLY LOW TENDER
To: [Tenderer Name]
Via: [E-Procurement Portal]
Date: [Date]
Procurement Ref: [Reference Number / Title]

Dear Sir/Madam,

Following the evaluation of the financial proposals submitted for the above-referenced procurement, your total proposed price of [€ Amount] appears to be abnormally low in relation to the [works/supplies/services] requested. Your price is [X]% lower than the median of the other submitted bids, and [X]% lower than our internal pre-tender estimate.

In accordance with Article 69 of Directive 2014/24/EU and the tender instructions, we are required to request a written explanation of the prices or costs proposed in your tender.

Please provide detailed evidence regarding:
1. The economics of the manufacturing process, services provided, or construction method.
2. The technical solutions chosen or any exceptionally favorable conditions available to you for the execution of the contract.
3. Confirmation and evidence of your compliance with applicable obligations in the fields of environmental, social, and labor law, including adherence to relevant collective agreements for staff working on this contract.
4. [Optional: Ask for breakdown of specific line items causing concern].

Please submit your comprehensive response by [Date/Time]. Failure to provide a satisfactory explanation or failure to demonstrate compliance with labor laws will result in the mandatory rejection of your tender.

Sincerely,
[Name/Title, Contracting Authority]
```
