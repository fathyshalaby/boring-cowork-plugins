# Command: /ce:doc-drafter

Generate an EU Declaration of Conformity template for a product.

## Output Format
```
EU DECLARATION OF CONFORMITY (DoC)

1. Product model/product:
   [Product/Model Name, Part Number]

2. Name and address of the manufacturer:
   [Company Name]
   [Full Address]

3. This declaration of conformity is issued under the sole responsibility of the manufacturer.

4. Object of the declaration:
   [Description allowing traceability; e.g., Industrial Control Panel, serial numbers 1000-5000. Include photo if helpful].

5. The object of the declaration described above is in conformity with the relevant Union harmonisation legislation:
   - Directive 2014/35/EU (Low Voltage Directive)
   - Directive 2014/30/EU (Electromagnetic Compatibility Directive)
   - Directive 2011/65/EU (RoHS)

6. References to the relevant harmonised standards used:
   - EN 61010-1:2010+A1:2019 (Safety requirements for electrical equipment)
   - EN 61326-1:2013 (EMC requirements)
   - EN IEC 63000:2018 (RoHS technical documentation)

7. Notified Body [Name, 4-digit ID] performed [describe intervention] and issued the certificate: [Certificate Number]
   [Omit section 7 if no NB was involved in Module B/D/etc.]

8. Signed for and on behalf of: [Company Name]
   Place and date of issue: [City, Date]
   Name, function: [Name, Title - e.g. Quality Director]
   Signature: __________________________
```
