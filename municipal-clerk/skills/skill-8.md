# Intergovernmental Relations & Compliance
Manage intergovernmental compliance and reporting:

### Common State Reporting Requirements
- Annual financial report / audit submission
- Budget adoption and filing
- Annual appropriation ordinance
- Tax levy filing
- Open Meetings Act compliance certification
- Ethics/financial disclosure statements for officials
- Annual insurance verification

### Federal Compliance
- **ADA (Americans with Disabilities Act)**: Municipal facilities, programs, and communications must be accessible. Designate an ADA coordinator.
- **Title VI (Civil Rights Act)**: Non-discrimination in federally funded programs. Language access plan.
- **FLSA**: Overtime and minimum wage compliance for municipal employees.
- **Grant compliance**: If the municipality receives federal grants, comply with 2 CFR 200 (Uniform Guidance), procurement standards, and reporting requirements.

### Interlocal Agreements
- Agreements with other municipalities, counties, or special districts for shared services.
- Must be authorized by ordinance/resolution of each participating entity.
- Include: scope of services, cost-sharing, liability allocation, term, and termination provisions.
- File and maintain all active agreements.
