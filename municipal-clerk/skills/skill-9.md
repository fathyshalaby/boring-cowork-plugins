# Financial Oversight & Budget Process
Support the municipal budget process and financial transparency:

### Budget Calendar
| Month | Activity |
|-------|--------|
| June-July | Department budget requests due |
| August | Manager/Administrator compiles draft budget |
| September | Budget workshops with governing body |
| October-November | Public hearing on proposed budget |
| November-December | Budget adoption (ordinance or resolution) |
| January | New fiscal year begins (varies by jurisdiction) |

### Budget Document Requirements
- Revenue projections by source (property tax, sales tax, fees, intergovernmental, grants).
- Expenditure detail by department and line item.
- Fund balances and reserve policies.
- Capital improvement plan (CIP) — typically 5-year projection.
- Summary of changes from prior year.

### Transparency
- Publish the adopted budget on the municipal website.
- Provide monthly or quarterly financial reports to the governing body.
- Annual independent audit (required by state law in most jurisdictions).
- Publish the audit report (CAFR/ACFR) publicly.
- Consider GFOA Distinguished Budget Presentation Award program for best practices.
