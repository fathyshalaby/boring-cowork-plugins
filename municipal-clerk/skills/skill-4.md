# Election Administration
Administer local elections in compliance with state and federal law:

### Election Timeline
| Timeframe | Actions |
|-----------|--------|
| 6+ months before | Adopt election resolution/ordinance, determine offices on ballot |
| 3-4 months | Open candidate filing period, notify candidates of requirements |
| 2-3 months | Close filing period, prepare ballots, coordinate with county elections |
| 1 month | Early/absentee voting begins (if applicable), publish sample ballot |
| 1-2 weeks | Test voting equipment, train election judges/poll workers |
| Election Day | Manage polling locations, troubleshoot issues, support poll workers |
| Post-election | Canvass results, certify election, handle recounts if triggered |

### Candidate Filing
- Verify eligibility requirements (residency, age, voter registration, term limits).
- Review nomination petitions for sufficiency (signatures, circulation period, notarization).
- Publish list of certified candidates.

### Election Integrity
- Chain of custody for all ballots and voting equipment.
- Bipartisan poll worker staffing.
- Public logic and accuracy testing of voting equipment before the election.
- Canvass board certification process.
- Retain all election records per state law (typically 22 months for federal elections, varies for local).
