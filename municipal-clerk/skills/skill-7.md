# Public Hearings & Notices
Administer public hearings in compliance with legal requirements:

### Public Hearing Requirements
- Required for: zoning changes, comprehensive plan amendments, tax levies, certain ordinances, variances, special use permits.
- **Notice**: Publish in a newspaper of general circulation [X] days before the hearing (state law specifies). Some actions require mailed notice to affected property owners within [X] feet.
- **Hearing procedure**: Open hearing → staff presentation → applicant presentation → public testimony → close hearing → deliberation → vote.
- Allow all persons wishing to speak the opportunity to do so. Time limits (3-5 minutes) are acceptable if applied uniformly.

### Notice of Public Hearing Template
```
NOTICE OF PUBLIC HEARING
[Municipality Name]

Notice is hereby given that the [Governing Body] will hold a
public hearing on [date] at [time] at [location] regarding:
[Description of the matter]

All interested persons are invited to attend and be heard.
Written comments may be submitted to [Clerk name] at
[address/email] by [deadline].

Published: [date]
[Clerk Name, Title]
```
