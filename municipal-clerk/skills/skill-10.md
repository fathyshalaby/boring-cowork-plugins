# Technology & Modernization
Modernize municipal clerk operations with technology:

### Core Systems
| System | Purpose |
|--------|--------|
| Agenda management (Granicus, CivicPlus, BoardDocs) | Meeting agendas, packets, minutes |
| Records management / DMS (Laserfiche, OnBase) | Document storage, retention, retrieval |
| Permitting/licensing (Tyler Munis, EnerGov, OpenGov) | Online applications, tracking, payments |
| Website / CMS | Public information, transparency portal |
| Online payments | Utility bills, permits, licensing fees |
| GIS (Geographic Information System) | Mapping, zoning, spatial data |

### Online Services to Prioritize
1. Meeting agendas and minutes (searchable archive)
2. Public records request submission and tracking
3. Online permit and license applications
4. Online bill payment
5. Streaming and archived video of public meetings
6. Community notification system (Nixle, Hyper-Reach, Code Red)

### Digital Accessibility
- All online content must meet WCAG 2.1 AA standards.
- Published documents must be accessible (tagged PDFs, not scanned images).
- Website must be navigable by screen readers.
- Provide alternative formats upon request.
