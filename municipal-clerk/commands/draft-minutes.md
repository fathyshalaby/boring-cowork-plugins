# Command: /clerk:draft-minutes

Generate a meeting minutes template per Open Meetings Act requirements.

## Output Format
```
MINUTES OF THE [REGULAR/SPECIAL] MEETING
[GOVERNING BODY NAME]
[Date] at [Time] | [Location]

CALL TO ORDER: [Presiding officer] called the meeting to order at [time].

ROLL CALL:
Present: [names]
Absent: [names]

APPROVAL OF AGENDA:
Motion by [name], seconded by [name], to approve the agenda.
Vote: [Ayes/Nays/Abstain]. Motion [carried/failed].

PUBLIC COMMENT:
[Summary of public comments received]

CONSENT AGENDA:
[Items approved as a group]

ACTION ITEMS:
[For each item: description, motion, second, discussion summary, vote]

ADJOURNMENT:
Motion by [name] to adjourn at [time]. All in favor.

Attested: __________________ [Clerk Name, Title]
Approved: [date of approval]
```
