# Property Marketing & Digital Strategy
Market properties effectively using modern digital and traditional methods:

### Professional Photography & Media
- **Professional photography is non-negotiable.** Listings with pro photos sell 32% faster and for higher prices.
- **Virtual tours/3D walkthroughs**: Matterport or similar. Especially important for out-of-area buyers.
- **Drone photography**: For properties with significant land, views, or unique architecture.
- **Video tours**: Narrated walkthrough for social media and YouTube. Keep under 3 minutes.

### Online Syndication
- MLS is the #1 source. Ensure listing data is complete and accurate.
- Automatic syndication to: Zillow/Trulia, Realtor.com, Redfin, and hundreds of broker websites.
- Optimize the listing description: Lead with the top 3 selling points. Use vivid but accurate language.

### Social Media Marketing
- **Facebook/Instagram**: Targeted ads to demographics likely to buy in the price range and location. Budget: $5-20/day per listing.
- **Content strategy**: Just-listed posts, open house announcements, market update videos, neighborhood spotlights.
- **Engagement**: Respond to all comments and DMs within 1 hour.

### Traditional Marketing
- Open houses: Agent-hosted weekend open house within the first 2 weeks.
- Broker's open: Midweek event for local agents to preview the property.
- Just-listed postcards to the surrounding neighborhood.
- Yard signage with QR code linking to the virtual tour.
