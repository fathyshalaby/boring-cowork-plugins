# Market Analysis & Comparative Pricing
Perform accurate market analysis using real estate fundamentals:

### CMA (Comparative Market Analysis) Methodology
1. **Select comparables**: 3-6 recently sold properties within 0.5-1 mile, similar size (±20% SF), same property type, sold within the last 6 months.
2. **Adjust for differences**: Add/subtract value for differences in square footage ($50-150/SF depending on market), bedrooms, bathrooms, garage, lot size, condition, and upgrades.
3. **Price per square foot**: Calculate the adjusted $/SF for each comp and derive a range.
4. **DOM (Days on Market)**: Average DOM tells you market speed. <30 days = seller's market. >90 days = buyer's market.
5. **List-to-sale ratio**: If homes sell for 98%+ of list price, the market is strong. Below 95% indicates negotiation room.

### Key Market Indicators
- **Months of inventory**: Active listings ÷ monthly sales. <3 months = seller's market, 3-6 = balanced, >6 = buyer's market.
- **Absorption rate**: Number of homes sold per month in a specific area.
- **Price trends**: Track median sale price month-over-month and year-over-year.
- **Interest rate impact**: Every 1% rate increase reduces buying power by approximately 10%.

### Pricing Strategy
- **Pricing to sell quickly**: Price at or slightly below the CMA range. Generates multiple offers.
- **Aspirational pricing**: 5-10% above CMA. Risks sitting on market and eventual price reduction.
- **Price reductions**: If no offers in 2-3 weeks in an active market, recommend a price adjustment. Each week on market reduces perceived value.
