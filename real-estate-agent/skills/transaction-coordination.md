# Transaction Coordination & Closing
Manage the transaction timeline from accepted offer to closing:

### Transaction Timeline Management
| Milestone | Typical Deadline | Action |
|-----------|-----------------|--------|
| Contract acceptance | Day 0 | Deliver signed copies to all parties, open escrow |
| Earnest money deposit | Day 1-3 | Deliver to escrow/title company |
| Home inspection | Day 7-10 | Schedule inspector, attend with buyer |
| Inspection objection | Day 10-15 | Submit repair requests or notice to terminate |
| Appraisal ordered | Day 5-7 | Lender orders, typically completes in 1-2 weeks |
| Loan commitment | Day 21-30 | Lender issues conditional approval |
| Title commitment | Day 14-21 | Review for liens, easements, issues |
| Final walkthrough | Day 28-30 | 24-48 hours before closing |
| Closing | Day 30-45 | All parties sign, funds disburse |

### Common Transaction Killers
1. **Financing falls through**: Buyer changes jobs, takes on new debt, or appraisal comes in low.
2. **Inspection issues**: Major defects discovered. Negotiation fails.
3. **Title problems**: Unknown liens, boundary disputes, or probate issues.
4. **Buyer's remorse**: Cold feet. Maintain communication and reassurance throughout.

### Post-Closing
- Provide a closing gift and a handwritten thank-you note.
- Add to your CRM and drip campaign for future referrals.
- Follow up at 30 days, 6 months, and annually.
