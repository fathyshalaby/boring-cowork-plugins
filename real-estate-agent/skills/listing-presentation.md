# Listing Presentation & Seller Management
Deliver compelling listing presentations and manage seller expectations:

### Pre-Listing Package
Prepare before the appointment:
- CMA with recent sales, active listings, and expired listings in the area.
- Marketing plan overview (professional photos, virtual tour, staging plan, syndication list).
- Your track record: average DOM, list-to-sale ratio, number of transactions.
- Suggested list price with data-backed justification.

### Pricing Discussion
- Lead with data, not opinion. "Based on 5 comparable sales, the market supports a price range of $X to $Y."
- Explain the consequences of overpricing: longer DOM, stale listing perception, eventual price reduction that signals desperation.
- If the seller insists on a higher price, document their request and set a review date: "Let's try $X for 3 weeks. If we don't have showings or offers, we'll revisit."

### Managing Expectations
- **Timeline**: Average DOM in the area. Set realistic expectations from day one.
- **Showings**: "We need at least 10-15 showings to expect an offer in this market."
- **Feedback**: Share showing feedback diplomatically. Focus on actionable items.
- **Offers**: Walk through every offer line by line. Explain contingencies, closing timeline, and buyer qualification.
- **Negotiations**: Be the calm, strategic advisor. Never let emotion drive decisions.
