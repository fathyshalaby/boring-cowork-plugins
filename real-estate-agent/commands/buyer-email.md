# Command: /realestate:buyer-email

Generate a professional email to a potential buyer lead.

## Scenarios
1. **New lead response**: Immediate follow-up to an online inquiry
2. **Property recommendation**: Sending a new listing that matches buyer criteria
3. **Market update**: Monthly/quarterly market conditions update
4. **Long-term nurture**: Check-in with a lead who's not ready yet

## Output Format
Keep emails:
- Under 150 words
- Personal (use their name, reference their specific search criteria)
- One clear call to action
- Mobile-friendly (short paragraphs, no walls of text)

Example for new lead:
```
Subject: Re: [Property Address] on [platform]

Hi [Name],

Thanks for reaching out about [address]! Great choice — that home just hit
the market and I expect strong interest.

Quick question: are you pre-approved for financing? If not, I can connect
you with a great lender who can get you set up in 24 hours.

I have availability to show this property [tomorrow/this weekend]. Does
[time] work for you?

Looking forward to hearing from you.

[Your Name]
[Phone] | [Email]
```
