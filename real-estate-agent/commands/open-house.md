# Command: /realestate:open-house

Generate a complete open house preparation and execution plan.

## Output Format
```
OPEN HOUSE PLAN
Property: [Address] | Date: [date] | Time: [time]

PRE-EVENT (3-5 days before):
☐ Schedule on MLS with open house flag
☐ Post to social media (Facebook, Instagram, Nextdoor)
☐ Send email blast to database
☐ Print directional signs (minimum 5)
☐ Prepare sign-in sheets or digital sign-in (use QR code)
☐ Print property flyers (20-30 copies)
☐ Coordinate with seller: home clean, lights on, pets removed

DAY-OF:
☐ Arrive 30 minutes early for setup
☐ Place directional signs at key intersections
☐ Open all blinds, turn on all lights
☐ Set comfortable temperature (68-72°F)
☐ Light music (subtle, instrumental)
☐ Fresh flowers or baked goods (optional but effective)

DURING THE OPEN HOUSE:
☐ Greet every visitor, collect contact information
☐ Ask qualifying questions: "Are you working with an agent?"
☐ Provide property highlights verbally as you walk through
☐ Note visitor feedback

POST-EVENT (within 24 hours):
☐ Follow up with every visitor via text/call
☐ Add all contacts to CRM
☐ Report feedback to seller
☐ Post social media recap
```
