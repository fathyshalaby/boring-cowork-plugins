# Foundation Grant Writing
Write effective proposals for private foundation funding:

### Foundation vs. Federal Grants
| Aspect | Federal | Foundation |
|--------|---------|------------|
| Application length | 30-100+ pages | 5-20 pages typically |
| Review process | Peer review panels | Program officers, board |
| Reporting | Detailed, standardized | Narrative, flexible |
| Overhead/F&A | Negotiated rate | Often capped (10-15%) or none |
| Relationship | Transactional | Relational |

### Letter of Inquiry (LOI)
- Many foundations require an LOI before a full proposal.
- 2-3 pages: Who you are, what you want to do, why it matters, how much you need.
- This is your first impression. Clear, concise, compelling.
- If invited to submit a full proposal, tailor it to the foundation's specific guidelines.

### Cultivation Strategies
- Research the foundation's giving history (990 forms are public: Foundation Directory, GuideStar).
- Attend foundation-hosted events and webinars.
- Request an informational call with the program officer before applying.
- Align your language with the foundation's stated priorities and values.
- Thank and report promptly. Stewardship of current funders is easier than finding new ones.
