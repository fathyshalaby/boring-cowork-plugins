# Post-Award Grant Management
Manage awarded grants for compliance and renewed funding:

### Post-Award Responsibilities
1. **Account setup**: Work with sponsored programs office to establish the grant account.
2. **Budget management**: Monitor spending against approved budget. Request budget modifications if needed (generally no prior approval needed for re-budgeting <25% of total award on federal grants).
3. **Hiring**: Recruit and hire personnel per the approved budget and timeline.
4. **Procurement**: Follow institutional and federal procurement policies. Sole-source justification for non-competitive purchases over $10K (2 CFR 200.320).
5. **Progress reporting**: Submit on time with substantive updates. Late reports can delay future funding.
6. **No-cost extensions**: If you need more time (but not more money), request a no-cost extension before the award end date.
7. **Closeout**: Reconcile all expenditures, submit final reports, return unspent funds.

### Budget Monitoring Best Practices
- Review monthly expenditure reports.
- Project forward: Will you underspend or overspend? Adjust hiring and procurement timelines.
- **Unobligated balance**: Agencies may reduce future funding if you consistently underspend.
- **Cost transfers**: Must be timely (within 90 days), well-documented, and justified. Pattern of late cost transfers raises audit flags.
