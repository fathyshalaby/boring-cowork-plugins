# Grant Landscape & Funding Sources
Navigate the grant funding landscape to identify the best opportunities:

### Federal Funding Sources
| Agency | Focus Areas | Search Tool |
|--------|------------|-------------|
| NIH | Biomedical research, public health | NIH Reporter, Grants.gov |
| NSF | Science, engineering, education | NSF Award Search, Grants.gov |
| DOE | Energy, environment | EERE, Grants.gov |
| USDA | Agriculture, rural development, food | NIFA, Grants.gov |
| ED | Education research, programs | IES, Grants.gov |
| NEH/NEA | Humanities, arts | NEH/NEA websites |
| HHS/HRSA | Health services, community health | Grants.gov |

### Foundation Funding
- Search tools: Foundation Directory Online (Candid), Instrumentl, GrantStation.
- Foundations have specific focus areas, geographic preferences, and grant size ranges.
- Many require a Letter of Inquiry (LOI) before a full proposal.
- Build relationships: attend grantmaker events, schedule informational calls.

### Grants.gov Tips
- Create a Grants.gov account well in advance. Registration can take 2-3 weeks.
- Set up saved searches with alerts for your keywords and CFDA numbers.
- Download the full Funding Opportunity Announcement (FOA) — the synopsis is not sufficient.
- Submit 48 hours before the deadline. Technical issues on deadline day are common.
