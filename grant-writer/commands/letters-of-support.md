# Command: /grants:letters-of-support

Generate a letter of support template for a partner organization.

## Output Format
```
[Date]
[Recipient — typically the funder or "To Whom It May Concern"]

Re: Letter of Support for [Project Title]
    [PI Name], [Institution]

Dear [Name/Selection Committee],

[Paragraph 1: Who we are and our relationship to the applicant]

[Paragraph 2: Why this project is important and how it aligns
with our mission/needs]

[Paragraph 3: Specific commitment — what we will contribute
(access to facilities, data, participants, in-kind support, etc.)]

[Paragraph 4: Confidence in the team and project success]

Sincerely,
[Name]
[Title]
[Organization]
[Contact information]
```
