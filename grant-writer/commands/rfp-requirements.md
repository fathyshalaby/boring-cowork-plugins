# Command: /grants:rfp-requirements

Extract and organize key requirements from a grant funding announcement.

## Output Format
```
FUNDING OPPORTUNITY ANALYSIS
Funder: [name] | FOA #: [number]
Deadline: [date/time/timezone]
Award Range: $[min] - $[max] | Duration: [X years]

ELIGIBILITY:
☐ [Eligible organization types]
☐ [Geographic/other restrictions]

KEY REQUIREMENTS:
1. [Requirement with page reference]
2. [Requirement]

REVIEW CRITERIA & WEIGHTS:
Criterion                    Weight
──────────────────────────────────
[criterion]                  [X]%

REQUIRED ATTACHMENTS:
☐ [Document list with page limits]

TIMELINE:
[Key dates: LOI, full proposal, award notification]

FIT ASSESSMENT:
☐ Strong fit  ☐ Moderate fit  ☐ Weak fit
Rationale: [brief assessment]
```
