# Command: /grants:budget-narrative

Generate a budget narrative justification for a grant proposal.

## Output Format
```
BUDGET JUSTIFICATION NARRATIVE
Project: [title] | Period: [dates] | Total: $[X]

A. PERSONNEL
[Name], [Title] — [X]% effort × $[salary] = $[X]
[Justification of this person's role in the project]

Fringe Benefits: [X]% × $[personnel total] = $[X]
[Institutional rate and components]

B. EQUIPMENT (>$5,000/unit)
[None requested / Item, cost, justification]

C. TRAVEL
[Purpose, destination, # trips, airfare + per diem calculation]

D. SUPPLIES
[Itemized list with justification]

E. CONTRACTUAL/SUBAWARDS
[Subrecipient, scope, budget, justification]

F. OTHER DIRECT COSTS
[Participant support, publication costs, etc.]

G. INDIRECT COSTS (F&A)
MTDC Base: $[X] × Rate: [X]% = $[X]
[Negotiated rate agreement reference]

TOTAL: $[X]
```
