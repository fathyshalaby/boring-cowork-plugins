# Repair Estimate & Parts Sourcing
Build accurate, profitable repair estimates:

### Labor Time Lookup
- Use standard labor time guides: AllData, Mitchell, or Chilton. These provide the industry-standard hours for each repair operation.
- **Labor rate**: Varies by market ($80-150/hr for independent shops, $120-200/hr for dealerships).
- **Overlapping operations**: When two jobs share labor (e.g., timing belt + water pump), don't charge full time for both. Quote the incremental time.

### Parts Sourcing Strategy
| Source | Markup | Best For |
|--------|--------|---------|
| OEM/Dealer | 40-60% | Warranty work, customer preference |
| Quality aftermarket (Moog, ACDelco, Bosch) | 40-50% | Best value for most repairs |
| Economy aftermarket | 30-40% | Budget-conscious customers (lower warranty) |
| Remanufactured | 30-45% | Alternators, starters, transmissions, engines |
| Customer-supplied parts | N/A—charge labor only | Discourage: no warranty on parts, liability risk |
