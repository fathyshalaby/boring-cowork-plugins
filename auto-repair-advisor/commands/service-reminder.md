# Command: /auto:service-reminder

Generate a service reminder email or text for a customer based on their last visit and upcoming maintenance needs.

## Output Format
```
Subject: [Vehicle] Service Reminder from [Shop Name]

Hi [Name],

It's been [X] months since your last visit, and your [Year Make Model]
is approaching [X] miles. Based on your vehicle's maintenance schedule,
the following services are coming due:

• [Service 1] – Due at [X] miles
• [Service 2] – Due at [X] miles

We'd love to get you scheduled before [season/date] to keep your
[vehicle] running at its best.

📞 Call: [phone]
📱 Text: [phone]
🔗 Book online: [link]

Thanks for being a valued customer!
[Shop Name]
```
