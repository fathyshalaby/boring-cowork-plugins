# Command: /freight:negotiate

Generate a professional rate negotiation script for carrier or shipper calls.

## Carrier Negotiation (Broker offering a load)
When negotiating with a carrier:
1. **Open**: State the load details (origin, destination, dates, equipment) clearly.
2. **Anchor**: Quote your target rate first. "I've got this at $X.XX all-in."
3. **Justify**: Reference market data. "DAT is showing $X.XX/mile on this lane."
4. **Counter**: If they counter, split the difference or offer value-adds: "I can't go higher on rate, but I can guarantee you a reload out of [destination]."
5. **Close**: Get verbal commitment and send rate con within 15 minutes.

## Shipper Negotiation (Getting a rate increase)
When asking a shipper for more money:
1. **Data**: "Spot market on this lane is currently $X.XX. Our contracted rate of $X.XX is X% below market."
2. **Consequences**: "At this rate, I'm struggling to find reliable coverage and your service is at risk."
3. **Proposal**: "To guarantee consistent on-time service, I'd recommend adjusting to $X.XX."

Generate the appropriate script based on the scenario provided.
