# Command: /food:sanitation-protocol

Generate a sanitation Standard Operating Procedure for a specific area or piece of equipment.

## Output Format
```
SANITATION SOP
Area/Equipment: [name] | SOP#: [number]
Frequency: [Daily/Weekly/Monthly] | Effective: [date]

REQUIRED CHEMICALS:
[Chemical, dilution ratio, contact time]

REQUIRED PPE:
[Gloves, eye protection, apron, etc.]

PROCEDURE:
1. [Step-by-step cleaning instructions]
2. [Continue steps]

VERIFICATION:
☐ Visual inspection — surfaces clean, no residue
☐ Sanitizer concentration test: [X] ppm (acceptable range)
☐ ATP swab result: [X] RLU (pass: <[threshold])

Documented By: _______ Date: _______
```
