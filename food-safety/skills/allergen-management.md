# Allergen Management
Prevent allergen cross-contact and ensure proper labeling:

### Major Food Allergens (US — FALCPA + FASTER Act)
1. Milk
2. Eggs
3. Fish
4. Crustacean shellfish
5. Tree nuts
6. Peanuts
7. Wheat
8. Soybeans
9. **Sesame** (added 2023 via FASTER Act)

### Allergen Control Program
- **Ingredient review**: Review every ingredient and supplier COA for allergen declarations. Hidden allergens in spice blends, sauces, and coatings.
- **Labeling**: "Contains" statement listing all major allergens. "May contain" (advisory) for potential cross-contact.
- **Production scheduling**: Run allergen-free products first, allergen-containing products last. Clean between changeovers.
- **Cleaning validation**: Verify allergen removal after cleaning with visual inspection + rapid allergen test kits (lateral flow devices).
- **Staff training**: All production and kitchen staff trained on allergen awareness. Retrain annually.

### Cross-Contact Prevention
- Dedicated utensils, cutting boards, and prep areas for allergen-free items.
- Color-coded equipment (e.g., purple = allergen-free).
- Separate storage for allergenic ingredients.
- Handwashing between handling allergenic and allergen-free foods.
