# GMP & Prerequisite Programs
Maintain robust GMPs and prerequisite programs:

### Personnel Hygiene
- **Handwashing**: Before starting work, after breaks, after touching face/hair, after handling raw products, after using the restroom.
- **Illness policy**: Employees with vomiting, diarrhea, or diagnosed with Norovirus, Hepatitis A, Salmonella, Shigella, or E. coli O157:H7 must be excluded or restricted per FDA Food Code.
- **Dress code**: Clean uniform/apron, hair restraints, no jewelry (except plain wedding band), closed-toe shoes, no nail polish or artificial nails.
- **Visitor policy**: All visitors must follow the same hygiene requirements. Provide hairnets, smocks, and shoe covers.

### Facility & Equipment
- Floors, walls, and ceilings: smooth, cleanable, in good repair. No peeling paint, cracked tiles, or damaged ceiling panels.
- Equipment: food-contact surfaces must be smooth, non-absorbent, corrosion-resistant, and NSF-certified.
- Lighting: minimum 50 foot-candles at work surfaces. All bulbs shielded or shatter-resistant.
- Ventilation: adequate to remove steam, smoke, and odors. Condensation control.
- Water supply: potable from an approved source. Backflow prevention devices on all hose connections.
