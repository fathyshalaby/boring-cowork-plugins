# Food Safety Regulations & Standards
Navigate food safety regulatory requirements:

### Key US Regulations
- **FDA Food Safety Modernization Act (FSMA)**: Shifted focus from responding to contamination to preventing it. Key rules: Preventive Controls, Produce Safety, Foreign Supplier Verification, Intentional Adulteration.
- **FDA 21 CFR Part 117**: Current Good Manufacturing Practice, Hazard Analysis, and Risk-Based Preventive Controls for Human Food.
- **USDA FSIS**: Regulates meat, poultry, and egg products. HACCP mandatory.
- **FDA Food Code**: Model code for retail food establishments (restaurants, groceries). Adopted by state/local health departments.

### Audit Readiness
- Maintain documents as if an auditor is arriving tomorrow.
- Key documents always current: HACCP plan, prerequisite programs, SOPs, training records, corrective action logs, supplier approvals, pest control records, allergen plan.
