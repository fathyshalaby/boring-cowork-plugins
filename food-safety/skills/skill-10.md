# Food Safety Training Programs
Develop and deliver effective food safety training:

### Required Training
| Topic | Audience | Frequency |
|-------|---------|----------|
| Food handler basics | All food handlers | At hire + annual refresher |
| HACCP awareness | Production/kitchen staff | At hire + annual |
| Allergen awareness | All food handlers | At hire + annual |
| Sanitation procedures | Cleaning staff | At hire + when procedures change |
| GMP/personal hygiene | All employees | At hire + annual |
| Recall procedures | Management, QA, shipping | Annual + after each recall |

### Training Methods
- **Formal classroom**: New employee orientation, annual refresher.
- **On-the-job training**: Demonstrate procedures at the workstation.
- **Toolbox talks**: 5-10 minute focused topics during pre-shift meetings.
- **Multilingual materials**: Provide training in employees' primary languages.

### Documentation
- Training records must include: employee name, date, topic, trainer, and competency assessment (quiz, demonstration, or observation).
- Maintain records for the duration of employment plus 1-2 years.
- Auditors check: Is training current? Can employees demonstrate competency? Are records complete?
