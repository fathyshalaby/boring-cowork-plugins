### Cleaning vs. Sanitizing
- **Clean**: Remove visible soil and food residue. Hot water + detergent.
- **Rinse**: Remove detergent residue.
- **Sanitize**: Reduce pathogens to safe levels. Chemical (quat 200 ppm or bleach 50-100 ppm) or heat (171°F for 30 seconds in a dish machine).
- **Test strips**: Verify sanitizer concentration at each use. Document.

### CIP (Clean in Place)
- For closed systems (tanks, pipes, fillers). Automated rinse → caustic wash → rinse → acid wash → rinse → sanitize cycle.
- Validate CIP effectiveness: visual inspection of accessible points, ATP swabs, microbial verification.
