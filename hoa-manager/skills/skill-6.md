# Insurance & Risk Management
Manage HOA insurance program and mitigate organizational risk:

### Required Insurance
| Coverage | What It Covers |
|----------|---------------|
| General Liability | Bodily injury/property damage in common areas |
| Property (Master Policy) | Common area buildings, structures, fixtures |
| Directors & Officers (D&O) | Board members' decisions and actions |
| Fidelity Bond/Crime | Theft/embezzlement of HOA funds |
| Workers' Comp | HOA employees (if any) |
| Umbrella | Excess liability above primary policies |

### D&O Insurance
- Protects board members from personal liability for decisions made in their fiduciary capacity.
- **Essential**: Without D&O, board members' personal assets are at risk in lawsuits.
- Coverage should include: defense costs, wrongful acts, employment practices, discrimination claims.

### Risk Mitigation
- Regular common area safety inspections.
- Prompt repair of trip hazards, broken lighting, and pool safety issues.
- Maintain adequate insurance limits. Review annually with your insurance agent.
- Keep accurate records of all maintenance, inspections, and safety improvements.
- Incident reports for any injury on common area property.
