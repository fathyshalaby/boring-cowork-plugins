# Communication & Community Relations
Build a well-informed, engaged community:

### Communication Channels
| Channel | Best For | Frequency |
|---------|---------|----------|
| Email/newsletter | General updates, events, reminders | Monthly |
| Community website/portal | Document access, forms, payments, announcements | Always available |
| Physical notices | Urgent issues, meeting notices, legal requirements | As needed |
| Annual meeting | Budget review, elections, major updates | Annually |
| Social media (private group) | Community engagement, event photos, quick polls | Ongoing |

### Meeting Communication
- Meeting notice per governing documents and state law (typically 10-14 days advance notice).
- Agenda distributed with the notice.
- Meeting minutes drafted within 30 days and approved at the next meeting.
- Make approved minutes available to all owners.

### Handling Complaints
1. Acknowledge receipt within 24-48 hours.
2. Investigate the issue.
3. Respond with findings and action taken (or planned) within 7-10 business days.
4. Document the complaint and resolution.
5. Follow up to ensure the issue is resolved.
