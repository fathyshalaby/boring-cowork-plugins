# Maintenance & Common Area Management
Maintain common areas to preserve property values and resident satisfaction:

### Maintenance Categories
- **Routine**: Scheduled, recurring tasks (landscaping, pool maintenance, common area cleaning).
- **Preventive**: Proactive tasks to extend asset life (gutter cleaning, HVAC servicing, seal coating).
- **Corrective**: Reactive repairs when something breaks (plumbing leak, fence damage, pothole).
- **Capital improvement**: Major projects funded from reserves (roof replacement, parking lot repaving).

### Vendor Management
- Obtain 3 competitive bids for any project over $[threshold per governing documents].
- Verify: licensed, bonded, insured (general liability + workers' comp). Request COI naming the HOA as additional insured.
- Written contracts with scope, timeline, payment terms, warranty, and insurance requirements.
- Site supervision for major projects. Document progress with photos.

### Maintenance Inspection Schedule
| Area | Frequency | Key Items |
|------|-----------|----------|
| Grounds/landscaping | Weekly | Irrigation, mowing quality, tree health |
| Building exterior | Monthly | Paint, siding, gutters, roof (visual) |
| Pool/amenities | Daily (in season) | Chemical levels, safety equipment, cleanliness |
| Parking/roads | Monthly | Potholes, striping, signage, lighting |
| Playground | Monthly | Equipment condition, surfacing, hardware |
| Common buildings | Monthly | HVAC, plumbing, fire safety, cleanliness |
