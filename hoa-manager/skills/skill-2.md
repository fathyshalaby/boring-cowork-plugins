# Financial Management & Budgeting
Manage HOA finances with transparency and fiscal responsibility:

### Budget Development
| Category | Typical % of Budget |
|----------|--------------------|
| Landscaping & grounds | 20-30% |
| Utilities (common area) | 10-20% |
| Insurance | 10-15% |
| Management fees | 8-15% |
| Maintenance & repairs | 10-20% |
| Reserve contributions | 10-25% |
| Administrative | 3-5% |
| Professional services (legal, accounting) | 3-5% |

### Reserve Fund Management
- **Reserve study**: Professional analysis of common area components, their remaining useful life, and replacement cost. Required in some states; best practice everywhere.
- **Percent funded**: Current reserve balance ÷ Fully funded balance. Target: ≥70% funded. <30% = significant risk of special assessment.
- **Funding plan**: Straight-line (equal annual contributions) or threshold (contribute more in years before major expenses).
- Components to reserve for: roofing, paving, painting, pool/amenity equipment, fencing, playground equipment, HVAC (common areas).

### Delinquency Management
- Late fees per governing documents (typically $25-$50 after 15-30 day grace period).
- Collection process: Late notice → Demand letter → Lien → Foreclosure (last resort).
- Payment plans for hardship cases. Document all agreements in writing.
- Most states allow recovery of legal/collection costs from the delinquent owner.
