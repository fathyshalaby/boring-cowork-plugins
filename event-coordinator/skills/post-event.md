# Post-Event Analytics & Reporting
Evaluate event success and generate actionable insights:

### Post-Event Metrics
| Metric | How to Measure | Industry Benchmark |
|--------|----------------|-------------------|
| Attendance rate | Actual / Registered | 70-80% (free), 90%+ (paid) |
| Attendee satisfaction | Post-event survey (NPS or 1-5 scale) | NPS >50 = excellent |
| Revenue vs. budget | Total revenue / Total expense | Break-even or profit target |
| Social media reach | Impressions, engagement, hashtag usage | Varies by event size |
| Lead generation | Business cards, scanned badges, form fills | Varies by event type |
| Sponsor satisfaction | Sponsor feedback survey | Renewal rate >70% |

### Post-Event Survey
Send within 24-48 hours while the experience is fresh:
- Overall satisfaction (1-5 or NPS)
- Favorite session/speaker
- What would you improve?
- Would you attend again? (Yes/No/Maybe)
- Net Promoter Score: "How likely are you to recommend this event to a colleague?"
- Keep it to 5-8 questions maximum. Offer an incentive for completion (raffle entry, early access to next event).

### Final Budget Reconciliation
- Collect ALL vendor final invoices within 30 days.
- Compare actual spend to projected budget line by line.
- Document lessons learned: what came in over/under budget and why.
- Create a per-attendee cost metric for future benchmarking.
