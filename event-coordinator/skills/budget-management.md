# Event Budget Management
Create and manage event budgets with precision:

### Budget Categories
| Category | Typical % of Total | Key Line Items |
|----------|-------------------|---------------|
| Venue | 25-35% | Room rental, security deposits, parking |
| Food & Beverage | 25-40% | Catering, bar service, linens, staffing |
| Audio/Visual | 10-15% | PA system, projector, lighting, staging |
| Entertainment | 5-15% | Speakers, bands, DJs, performers |
| Decor & Florals | 5-10% | Centerpieces, backdrop, signage, linens |
| Marketing & Print | 5-10% | Invitations, programs, banners, social ads |
| Photography/Video | 3-5% | Photographer, videographer, photo booth |
| Staffing | 3-5% | Event coordinators, registration desk, security |
| Contingency | 5-10% | **Always include.** Unexpected costs WILL arise. |

### Budget Rules
- Get **3 quotes minimum** for every vendor category over $1,000.
- Track actual spend against budget in real-time. Update after every vendor payment.
- **Hidden costs** to watch for: Service charges (18-22% on catering), corkage fees, overtime charges, setup/teardown fees, electrical hookup charges, and parking fees.
- Negotiate: Ask for package deals, off-peak discounts, and included setup/teardown.
