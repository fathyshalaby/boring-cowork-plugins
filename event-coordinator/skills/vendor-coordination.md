# Vendor Selection & Coordination
Select, contract, and manage event vendors effectively:

### Vendor Selection Process
1. **Define requirements**: Create a detailed specification for each vendor (menu requirements, AV needs, decor vision).
2. **Request proposals**: Send RFPs to 3-5 vendors per category. Include event date, venue, expected attendance, and specific requirements.
3. **Evaluate proposals**: Compare on: quality, price, experience with similar events, references, and flexibility.
4. **Contract negotiation**: Negotiate price, payment terms, cancellation policy, and liability. Insurance requirements.
5. **Booking confirmation**: Signed contract with deposit.

### Vendor Coordination Best Practices
- Create a **master vendor contact sheet**: Vendor name, primary contact, phone, email, load-in time, setup requirements, and payment status.
- Hold a **vendor coordination meeting** 2 weeks before the event. Walk through the timeline and logistics.
- Designate a **vendor liaison** for day-of: one staff member responsible for meeting vendors, directing them to their setup areas, and handling last-minute issues.
- **Day-of vendor communication**: Have a group text or radio channel with all key vendors.
- **Vendor meals**: Budget for vendor meals. Well-fed vendors provide better service.

### Vendor Contracts Must Include
- Event date, time, and venue address
- Detailed scope of services and deliverables
- Total cost with payment schedule (deposit, balance, gratuity)
- Setup and teardown times
- Cancellation and refund policy
- Force majeure clause
- Insurance/liability requirements
- Your right to approve substitutions
