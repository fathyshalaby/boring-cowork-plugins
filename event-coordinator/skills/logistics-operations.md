# Event Logistics & Operations
Manage all operational logistics for seamless event execution:

### Venue Assessment Checklist
- [ ] Room capacity (seated, standing, classroom, theater)
- [ ] Layout flexibility (movable walls, built-in stage, dance floor)
- [ ] AV capabilities (in-house system, outside AV allowed, rigging points)
- [ ] Wi-Fi bandwidth (sufficient for attendee count + social media + livestream?)
- [ ] Restroom count (adequate for attendance?)
- [ ] Parking capacity (on-site, nearby garages, valet options)
- [ ] Accessibility (ADA compliance: ramps, elevators, accessible restrooms)
- [ ] Load-in/load-out logistics (loading dock, freight elevator, time restrictions)
- [ ] Noise restrictions (curfew hours, sound limits)
- [ ] Catering exclusivity (can you bring outside catering, or must you use in-house?)
- [ ] Insurance requirements (COI, additional insured)

### Floor Plan Design
- Use a floor plan tool (AllSeated, Social Tables, or even PowerPoint to scale).
- Leave adequate aisle space (minimum 36" for ADA, 48" preferred).
- Stage sightlines: Ensure all seats can see the stage/screen. No pillars blocking views.
- Registration/check-in: Near the entrance, with adequate queuing space for peak arrival times.
- Food & beverage: Position away from the main program area to minimize noise disruption.
- Emergency exits: Never block. Always have a clear evacuation plan.

### Transportation & Parking
- For hotel conferences: Room block management and shuttle coordination.
- For off-site events: Valet parking, shuttle buses from parking lots, rideshare coordination.
- Signage: Directional signage from parking to venue entrance to registration.
