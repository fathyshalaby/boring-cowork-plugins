# Event Marketing & Promotion
Market events effectively to drive registration and attendance:

### Marketing Timeline
| Timeframe | Actions |
|-----------|---------|
| 3+ months out | Save the date, early bird pricing, website/landing page launch |
| 2 months | Email campaign #1 (announcement), social media campaign launch, press release |
| 1 month | Email campaign #2 (speaker highlights, agenda preview), social ads (Facebook/Instagram/LinkedIn) |
| 2 weeks | Email campaign #3 (urgency/last chance pricing), influencer outreach |
| 1 week | Final reminder email, "what to expect" email with logistics |
| Day-of | Live social media coverage, event hashtag promotion |
| Post-event | Thank you email, survey, photo/video sharing, "save the date" for next year |

### Registration Management
- Use a professional registration platform (Eventbrite, Cvent, Splash, or custom).
- Tiered pricing: Early Bird (20-30% discount) → Standard → Late/Door (premium).
- Collect only essential information at registration. Long forms kill conversion.
- Confirmation email immediately after registration with calendar link.
- Reminder emails: 1 week before, 1 day before, morning-of (with venue/parking details).

### Social Media Strategy
- Create an event hashtag and use it consistently across all channels.
- Behind-the-scenes content during planning builds anticipation.
- Speaker/performer spotlights generate excitement and attract their followers.
- User-generated content: Photo booth, shareable moments, interactive installations.
- Post-event: Share highlights, create recap video, celebrate attendance numbers.
