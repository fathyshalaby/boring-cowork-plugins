# Guest Experience Design
Design memorable guest experiences from arrival to departure:

### Touchpoints
1. **Arrival**: Easy wayfinding, warm greeting, efficient check-in. First impressions are everything.
2. **Registration**: Fast, organized, and friendly. Pre-print name badges. Have a separate line for walk-ins.
3. **Opening moments**: The first 5 minutes set the tone. Strong music cue, warm welcome, high energy.
4. **Networking**: Don't leave networking to chance. Provide structured opportunities: icebreaker activities, assigned seating to mix groups, networking lounges with conversation prompts.
5. **Content/Program**: Engaging speakers, interactive elements (live polls, Q&A), and tightly managed schedule. Do NOT run over time.
6. **Food & Drink**: Quality matters. This is what people talk about after. Strategic timing of refreshments.
7. **Surprise moments**: A wow factor that attendees don't expect: surprise performer, swag bag with premium items, interactive installation.
8. **Departure**: Thank guests as they leave. Follow up within 24 hours with photos and a survey.

### Personalization
- Pre-event survey to learn attendee interests and dietary needs.
- Personalized name badges (large first name for easy networking).
- Tailored swag: Quality over quantity. One premium item beats five cheap ones.
- For VIPs: Personalized welcome note, reserved seating, exclusive access to speaker meet-and-greet.
