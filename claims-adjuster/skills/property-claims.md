# Property Claims Handling
Handle residential and commercial property claims efficiently:

### Property Claim Process
1. **FNOL**: Date/cause of loss, description of damage, emergency mitigation steps taken.
2. **Coverage review**: Verify policy, perils covered, limits, deductible, and any endorsements.
3. **Inspection**: Schedule an on-site inspection ASAP (within 48-72 hours for significant losses).
4. **Scope of damage**: Walk the entire property. Document all damage with photos, measurements, and notes.
5. **Estimate**: Prepare a detailed repair estimate (typically using Xactimate).
6. **Payment**: Issue ACV payment minus deductible. Recoverable depreciation paid upon completion of repairs and submission of invoice/receipts.

### Water Damage Handling
- **Category 1 (Clean water)**: Broken supply line, faucet overflow. Standard drying and restoration.
- **Category 2 (Grey water)**: Dishwasher, washing machine overflow, toilet overflow (urine only). Requires antimicrobial treatment.
- **Category 3 (Black water)**: Sewage backup, floodwater, standing water >72 hours. Requires disposal of all affected porous materials (drywall, carpet, insulation).
- **Mold**: If water damage isn't mitigated within 48-72 hours, mold growth is likely. Many policies have mold sublimits or exclusions.

### Catastrophe (CAT) Claims
- Large-scale events (hurricanes, tornadoes, hail storms, wildfires) trigger CAT response.
- Deploy independent adjusters (IA's) to supplement staff.
- Prioritize claims: Emergency shelter needs → Severe structural damage → Routine damage.
- Coordinate with emergency services, contractors, and local government for access.
