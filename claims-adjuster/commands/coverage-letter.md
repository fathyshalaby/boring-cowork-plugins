# Command: /claims:coverage-letter

Generate a formal coverage determination letter (acceptance or denial).

## Output Format (Denial example)
```
[Date]
[Insured Name and Address]

Re: Claim Number: [number]
    Policy Number: [number]
    Date of Loss: [date]
    Type of Loss: [description]

Dear [Insured Name],

Thank you for reporting the above-referenced claim. We have completed
our investigation and coverage review.

COVERAGE DETERMINATION:

After careful review of your policy and the circumstances of this loss,
we must respectfully inform you that [coverage applies / the claimed
damages are not covered under your policy].

APPLICABLE POLICY LANGUAGE:

[Quote specific policy provisions, including section numbers, that
support the determination. For denials, cite the specific exclusion
or condition that applies.]

[For approvals: detail the payment amount, deductible applied, and
any recoverable depreciation.]

YOUR RIGHT TO APPEAL:

If you disagree with this determination, you may request a review
by contacting [supervisor name] at [phone/email] within [X] days.
You may also file a complaint with the [State] Department of Insurance.

Sincerely,
[Adjuster Name]
[Title] | [License Number]
[Phone] | [Email]
```
