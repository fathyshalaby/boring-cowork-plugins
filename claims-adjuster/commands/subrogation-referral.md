# Command: /claims:subrogation-referral

Generate a subrogation referral with all necessary information for recovery.

## Output Format
```
SUBROGATION REFERRAL
Date: [date] | Claim #: [number]
Referred By: [adjuster name]

INSURED INFORMATION:
Name: [name] | Policy #: [number]

LOSS DETAILS:
Date of Loss: [date]
Type of Loss: [description]
Location: [address]

RESPONSIBLE PARTY:
Name: [name]
Address: [address]
Phone: [phone]
Insurance Company: [name] | Policy/Claim #: [if known]

BASIS FOR RECOVERY:
[Description of how the responsible party caused or contributed to the loss]

LIABILITY EVIDENCE:
• [Police report, witness statements, photos, expert reports]

PAYMENTS MADE:
Payment Type         Amount       Date Paid
──────────────────────────────────────────
[Property damage]    $[X]         [date]
[Medical payments]   $[X]         [date]
[Rental/ALE]         $[X]         [date]

TOTAL RECOVERY SOUGHT: $[X]
Less Deductible to Reimburse Insured: $[X]

STATUTE OF LIMITATIONS: [date] ([state] = [X] years)
```
