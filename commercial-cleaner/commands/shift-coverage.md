# Command: /cleaner:shift-coverage

Generate a weekly cleaning shift coverage schedule.

## Output Format
```
SHIFT COVERAGE SCHEDULE
Facility: [name] | Week of: [date]
Shift: [hours] | Required Staff: [X]

         MON    TUE    WED    THU    FRI    SAT    SUN
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Zone A:  [name] [name] [name] [name] [name] [name] [name]
Zone B:  [name] [name] [name] [name] [name] [name] [name]
Zone C:  [name] [name] [name] [name] [name] [name] [name]

COVERAGE GAPS: [any unfilled shifts]
ON-CALL: [name, phone]

SPECIAL INSTRUCTIONS:
• [Any special events, deep cleaning, or client requests this week]
```
