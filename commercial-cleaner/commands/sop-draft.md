# Command: /cleaner:sop-draft

Generate a detailed Standard Operating Procedure for a specific cleaning task.

## Output Format
```
STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE
SOP #: [number] | Version: [X] | Effective: [date]
Task: [description]
Applies To: [which areas/facility types]

REQUIRED SUPPLIES:
• [Supply list with specific products and quantities]

REQUIRED PPE:
• [PPE items required]

PROCEDURE:
Step 1: [Detailed instruction]
Step 2: [Detailed instruction]
[etc.]

SAFETY WARNINGS:
⚠️ [Chemical safety, slip hazards, or other risks]

QUALITY STANDARDS:
[What "done right" looks like for inspection purposes]

FREQUENCY: [Daily/Weekly/Monthly/As needed]
ESTIMATED TIME: [X] minutes per [unit]

Approved By: ______________ Date: __________
```
