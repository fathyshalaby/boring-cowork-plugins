# Command: /cleaner:client-response

Generate a professional response to a client cleaning complaint.

## Output Format
```
COMPLAINT RESPONSE

Date: [date]
To: [Client contact name]
Re: [Facility name] – Service Concern

Dear [Name],

Thank you for bringing this to our attention. We take all feedback
seriously and are committed to maintaining the highest standards
at your facility.

ISSUE REPORTED:
[Summary of the client's concern]

INVESTIGATION FINDINGS:
[What we found when we inspected/investigated]

CORRECTIVE ACTIONS TAKEN:
1. [Immediate action taken]
2. [Systemic improvement to prevent recurrence]

GOING FORWARD:
[Preventive measures and follow-up plan]

I will personally follow up on [date] to ensure the issue is
fully resolved. Please don't hesitate to reach out directly
at any time.

Sincerely,
[Account Manager Name]
[Phone] | [Email]
```
