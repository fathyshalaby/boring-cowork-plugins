# Floor Care & Maintenance
Master floor care techniques for all commercial flooring types:

### Hard Floor Maintenance Program
| Level | Procedure | Frequency |
|-------|-----------|-----------|
| Daily | Dust mop, damp mop with neutral cleaner | Daily |
| Interim | Spray buff or burnish to restore gloss | Weekly-Monthly |
| Periodic | Scrub and recoat (top scrub, apply 1-2 coats of finish) | Quarterly |
| Restorative | Strip all finish, clean, and apply 3-5 coats of finish | 1-2x per year |

### VCT (Vinyl Composition Tile) Floor Finish Program
1. **Stripping**: Apply stripper solution (1:4 dilution typical), allow 5-10 min dwell time, agitate with floor machine (175 RPM, black pad), wet vac, rinse, wet vac again.
2. **Sealing**: Apply 1-2 coats of floor sealer (if required by finish manufacturer). Allow to dry between coats.
3. **Finishing**: Apply 3-5 thin, even coats of floor finish. Use a clean microfiber mop or finish applicator. Allow 30-45 minutes dry time between coats. Do not apply thick coats—they yellow and powder.
4. **Burnishing**: After finishing, burnish with UHS (Ultra High Speed, 1500-2500 RPM) machine and white or natural hair pad for maximum gloss.

### Carpet Maintenance
| Level | Method | Frequency | Purpose |
|-------|--------|-----------|---------|
| Preventive | Walk-off mats, vacuuming | Daily | Remove 80% of dry soil |
| Interim | Bonnet cleaning or encapsulation | Monthly-Quarterly | Appearance maintenance |
| Restorative | Hot water extraction (HWE) | Semi-annually | Deep cleaning, removes trapped soil |

### Specialty Floors
- **Polished concrete**: Dust mop daily, auto-scrub with neutral cleaner, burnish as needed. No wax or finish.
- **Terrazzo**: Similar to VCT but can also be diamond-polished. Use pH-neutral cleaners only.
- **Natural stone (marble, granite)**: pH-neutral only. Acid damages marble. Seal annually.
- **Hardwood**: Dust mop, damp mop (barely damp—excess water damages wood). Screen and recoat annually.
