# Specialty Cleaning Services
Offer and perform specialty cleaning services beyond routine janitorial:

### Post-Construction Cleaning
- **Phase 1 (Rough clean)**: Remove large debris, sweep all floors, wipe ducts and surfaces.
- **Phase 2 (Detail clean)**: Scrub restrooms, dust all surfaces, clean light fixtures, wipe windows, detail kitchens.
- **Phase 3 (Final/Touch-up clean)**: Final window cleaning, floor finishing, punch list items.
- Charge premium rates (2-3x standard cleaning rate). Labor-intensive work.

### Hard Floor Restoration
- Strip existing finish (2-4 applications of stripper).
- Clean and neutralize the floor.
- Apply sealer (1-2 coats) and finish (3-5 coats).
- Burnish for maximum gloss.
- Price: $0.50-$2.00 per square foot depending on floor condition and number of coats.

### Window Cleaning (Interior/Exterior)
- Interior windows: Squeegee technique produces streak-free results. Use a strip washer with cleaning solution, then squeegee from top to bottom.
- Exterior windows (low-rise): Extension poles, water-fed pole systems.
- High-rise: Requires specialized equipment (bosun's chair, scaffolding) and trained personnel. Often subcontracted.

### Pandemic/Deep Disinfection
- Electrostatic sprayers apply disinfectant with a charged particle that wraps around surfaces for 360° coverage.
- Fogging: ULV (Ultra Low Volume) foggers disperse disinfectant as a fine mist. Effective for large areas.
- Always use an EPA-registered disinfectant with the appropriate contact time for the target pathogen.
