# Command: /inspect:maintenance-guide

Generate a seasonal maintenance guide for the homeowner based on the property's systems.

## Output Format
```
HOMEOWNER MAINTENANCE GUIDE
Property: [Address]

MONTHLY:
• Replace/check HVAC air filter
• Test smoke and CO detectors

QUARTERLY:
• Flush water heater (drain 1-2 gallons to remove sediment)
• Clean dryer vent

SEMI-ANNUALLY (Spring & Fall):
• HVAC professional tune-up (cooling in spring, heating in fall)
• Clean gutters and check downspout extensions
• Inspect caulking around windows, doors, and penetrations

ANNUALLY:
• Inspect roof for damage (or hire professional)
• Test water pressure (40-80 PSI normal)
• Service garage door springs and opener
• Inspect foundation perimeter for settlement cracks
• Trim vegetation 2+ feet from the house

EVERY 3-5 YEARS:
• Professional chimney sweep and inspection
• Sewer scope / drain camera inspection
• Re-caulk bathtub and shower surrounds
```
