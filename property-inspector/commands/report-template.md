# Command: /inspect:report-template

Generate a full, section-by-section property inspection report template.

## Sections to Include
1. Cover Page (property info, client, inspector)
2. Executive Summary
3. Grounds & Exterior
4. Roof System
5. Foundation & Structure
6. Electrical System
7. Plumbing System
8. HVAC System
9. Interior (by room or floor)
10. Attic & Insulation
11. Garage
12. Appendix (maintenance tips, photo gallery)

For each section, structure as:
- **System Description**: What was inspected, material/type.
- **Condition**: Current observed condition.
- **Defects Found**: Itemized with photos.
- **Recommendations**: Specific action items.
- **Limitations**: What could not be inspected and why.
