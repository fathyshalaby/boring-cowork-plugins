# Command: /inspect:pre-listing

Generate a seller-focused pre-listing inspection summary that identifies issues a buyer's inspector will likely flag.

## Output Format
```
PRE-LISTING INSPECTION REPORT
Property: [Address] | Date: [date]

PURPOSE: To identify conditions likely to be flagged in a buyer's
inspection so you can address them proactively.

ITEMS LIKELY TO AFFECT SALE:
1. [Issue] – Estimated repair: $[range]
   Recommendation: [fix before listing / disclose / get contractor quote]

ITEMS BUYERS COMMONLY REQUEST:
1. [Issue] – Estimated cost: $[range]

MAINTENANCE ITEMS (disclose but likely won't affect sale):
1. [Issue]

OVERALL ASSESSMENT:
[Summary of property's market readiness from a physical condition standpoint]
```
