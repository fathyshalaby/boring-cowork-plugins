# Registration & Enrollment Management
Administer the course registration process:

### Registration Process
1. **Schedule building**: Academic departments submit course offerings. Registrar assigns rooms, resolves conflicts.
2. **Priority registration**: Athletes, honors, disabilities, veterans, seniors (by credit hours).
3. **Open registration**: All eligible students register by class standing.
4. **Add/Drop period**: Students adjust schedules. Typically first 1-2 weeks.
5. **Late registration**: After add/drop, requires instructor and/or dean approval.
6. **Withdrawal period**: Students can withdraw with a W grade (no GPA impact) through a published deadline.

### Enrollment Limits & Capacity
- Room capacity drives section enrollment caps.
- Waitlist management: Auto-enroll from waitlist when seats open, or manual approval.
- Course overrides: Instructor consent, prerequisite override, capacity override. Document all overrides.

### Registration Holds
| Hold Type | Placed By | Prevents |
|-----------|----------|----------|
| Financial | Bursar | Registration, transcript release |
| Academic | Advisor | Registration (requires advising) |
| Disciplinary | Dean of Students | Registration |
| Immunization | Health services | Registration, housing |
| Library | Library | Graduation |
