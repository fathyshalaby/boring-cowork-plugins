# Degree Audit & Graduation
Manage degree requirements verification and graduation processing:

### Degree Audit Systems
- **Automated audit** (DegreeWorks, uAchieve, Stellic): Maps completed courses against degree requirements.
- **Requirement groups**: General education, major core, major electives, minor, free electives, total credits.
- **Course substitutions**: Documented approval required (department chair or dean). Enter into audit system with justification.
- **Exception processing**: For catalog year changes, program changes, study abroad credit, etc.

### Graduation Processing
1. Student applies for graduation by published deadline.
2. Run degree audit for all applicants.
3. Identify deficiencies and notify students.
4. Final audit after last semester grades are posted.
5. Degree conferral by the governing board/president.
6. Post degree to transcript. Order diplomas.
7. Report degrees to National Student Clearinghouse.

### Latin Honors Calculation
| Honor | Typical Threshold |
|-------|------------------|
| Summa Cum Laude | ≥3.90 GPA |
| Magna Cum Laude | ≥3.70 GPA |
| Cum Laude | ≥3.50 GPA |
- Verify minimum credit hours at the institution (typically 60+ hours in residence).
- Calculate based on ALL coursework or institutional GPA? Follow your catalog policy.
