# Student Information Systems (SIS)
Manage and optimize your Student Information System:

### Major SIS Platforms
| Platform | Type | Market |
|----------|------|--------|
| Ellucian Banner | On-premise/cloud | Large universities |
| Ellucian Colleague | On-premise/cloud | Community colleges |
| Workday Student | Cloud SaaS | Growing market |
| Oracle PeopleSoft Campus Solutions | On-premise/cloud | Large universities |
| Jenzabar | Cloud/on-premise | Small-mid private institutions |

### Data Governance
- Define data owners, data stewards, and data standards for student records.
- Data dictionary: Standardize field definitions across the institution.
- Data integrity audits: Regular checks for duplicate records, inconsistent data, and missing required fields.
- Security: Role-based access. Principle of least privilege. Annual access review.

### Integrations
- SIS ↔ LMS (Canvas, Blackboard): Enrollment sync, grade passback.
- SIS ↔ Financial aid system: Enrollment status, SAP status.
- SIS ↔ Clearinghouse: Enrollment and degree reporting.
- SIS ↔ DegreeWorks: Degree audit requirements and course history.
- Data warehouse/BI: Institutional research reporting.
