# Enrollment Reporting & Compliance
Meet enrollment reporting requirements:

### National Student Clearinghouse (NSC)
- Report enrollment status at least every 60 days (monthly preferred).
- Report: student identifier, name, DOB, enrollment status (F/H/L/W/G), enrollment dates, program.
- Errors flagged by NSC must be corrected promptly.
- Degree reporting: Report within 30 days of conferral.

### IPEDS (Integrated Postsecondary Education Data System)
- Mandatory for all Title IV institutions.
- Key surveys: Enrollment (12-month, fall), Completions, Graduation Rates, Financial Aid, HR, Finance.
- Data quality: Registrar provides enrollment, graduation, and retention data. Accuracy is critical — this data is publicly reported.

### VA Certification (GI Bill)
- Certifying Official must be trained and designated.
- Report enrollment, changes, and terminations to the VA via VA-ONCE.
- Verify satisfactory academic progress each term.
- Standard of Progress: Students must maintain satisfactory progress to continue receiving VA benefits.

### Title IV (Financial Aid Enrollment Reporting)
- Enrollment status changes affect financial aid eligibility.
- Report changes within 30 days to NSLDS (via Clearinghouse).
- Return of Title IV funds calculation when a student withdraws.
