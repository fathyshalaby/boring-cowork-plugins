# Commencement & Ceremonial Functions
Coordinate commencement ceremonies and related administrative functions:

### Commencement Planning
| Timeline | Tasks |
|----------|-------|
| 6 months | Venue booking, honorary degree nominations, speaker invitations |
| 4 months | Graduation application deadline, regalia ordering |
| 2 months | Degree audits for all applicants, program printing prep |
| 1 month | Finalize program, marshaling plan, rehearsal schedule |
| 1 week | Final degree list, seating charts, signage, AV check |
| Day of | Ceremony execution, photography, live streaming |
| After | Diploma mailing, degree posting, reporting |

### Diploma Production
- Order diplomas after final degree conferral (not before — students may not complete requirements).
- Verify every name spelling with the student before printing.
- Include: Student name (as recorded), degree title, major, honors, institutional seal, date of conferral, signatures.
- Replacement diploma process: Verify identity, charge replacement fee, issue with current date and signature.

### Academic Regalia
- Follow American Council on Education (ACE) guidelines for academic regalia.
- Gown, cap, and tassel color indicate degree level and institution.
- Hood colors indicate field of study and institution.
