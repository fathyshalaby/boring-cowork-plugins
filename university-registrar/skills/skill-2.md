# Academic Records & Transcripts
Manage academic records with accuracy and integrity:

### Transcript Standards
- **Official transcript**: Issued by the registrar with institutional seal/signature. Tamper-evident security paper or electronic with digital signature.
- **Unofficial transcript**: For student's personal use. No security features.
- **Electronic transcripts**: Via SPEEDE server, National Student Clearinghouse, Parchment, or Credential Solutions.

### Transcript Content
- Student name, ID, date of birth
- Institution name, CEEB/FICE code
- Enrollment history by term
- Courses: prefix, number, title, credit hours, grade
- Term and cumulative GPA
- Degrees conferred with date and honors
- Academic standing notations
- Transfer credit (institution, course equivalency)

### Records Integrity
- Only authorized staff may update academic records.
- All grade changes require documented approval (instructor signature, department chair approval for late changes).
- Audit trail: Every change to a student record is logged (who, what, when, why).
- Archival: Academic records are retained permanently. Retention schedule for supporting documents varies.
- Disaster recovery: Maintain offsite backup of all academic records.
