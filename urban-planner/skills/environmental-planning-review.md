# Environmental Planning & Review
Integrate environmental considerations into planning decisions:

### Environmental Review (NEPA/State equivalents)
- **Categorical Exclusion (CE)**: Actions that normally don't have significant environmental impact.
- **Environmental Assessment (EA)**: Determines whether an EIS is needed. Results in a FONSI (Finding of No Significant Impact) or triggers an EIS.
- **Environmental Impact Statement (EIS)**: Full analysis for actions with significant environmental impact.

### Environmental Constraints
| Constraint | Regulation | Planning Impact |
|-----------|-----------|----------------|
| Floodplain | FEMA/NFIP | No development in floodway, restricted in 100-yr floodplain |
| Wetlands | Clean Water Act §404 | Avoid, minimize, mitigate. Army Corps permit |
| Endangered species | ESA | Habitat conservation plans |
| Steep slopes (>25%) | Local ordinance | Building restrictions, erosion risk |
| Tree preservation | Local ordinance | Minimum tree canopy, replacement ratios |
| Stormwater | EPA MS4 permit | Post-development runoff ≤ pre-development |

### Green Infrastructure
- Bioswales, rain gardens, permeable pavement, green roofs, tree preservation.
- LID (Low Impact Development): Manage stormwater at the source.
- LEED-ND (Neighborhood Development) rating system for sustainable neighborhood planning.
