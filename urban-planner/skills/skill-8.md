# Economic Development Planning
Integrate economic development into the planning process:

### Economic Analysis
- **Market study**: Supply/demand analysis for retail, office, industrial, and residential uses.
- **Fiscal impact analysis**: Will the proposed development generate more tax revenue than it costs in services?
- **Economic impact analysis**: Jobs, income, and tax revenue generated by a project or program.

### Development Incentives
| Incentive | Mechanism |
|-----------|----------|
| TIF (Tax Increment Financing) | Increment in property tax revenue funds improvements |
| Enterprise zones | Tax credits for businesses in designated areas |
| Fee waivers | Reduce/eliminate impact fees, permit fees |
| Infrastructure investment | Public investment in roads, utilities, broadband |
| Brownfield remediation grants | Clean up contaminated sites for redevelopment |
| Public-private partnerships | Shared investment and risk |

### Redevelopment Planning
- Identify underperforming areas: vacant properties, brownfields, declining commercial corridors.
- Create a redevelopment plan with a vision, land use plan, infrastructure plan, and implementation strategy.
- Engage property owners and potential developers early.
- Use form-based codes or design guidelines to ensure quality redevelopment.
