# Command: /mobility:art16-draft

Draft the business justification for an Article 16 exception request.

## Output Format
```
ARTICLE 16 EXCEPTION REQUEST: BUSINESS JUSTIFICATION
Employee: [Name]
Request: Keep employee in [UK] scheme while exclusively working in [Spain] for 3 years.

1. FACTS OF THE ASSIGNMENT
[The employee, a senior aerospace engineer, is being assigned from the UK parent to the Spanish subsidiary to oversee the construction of a new manufacturing line. The projected timeline is 36 months, exceeding the standard 24-month posting limit.]

2. EMPLOYEE INTEREST
[The employee is 58 years old and heavily invested in the UK State Pension system. Switching to the Spanish system for a temporary 3-year period would severely disrupt their pension accrual close to retirement. The employee explicitly consents to and requests remaining in the UK scheme.]

3. EMPLOYER INTEREST
[Maintaining the employee in the UK system drastically reduces administrative burden, avoiding the need to establish a shadow payroll in Spain solely for one temporary expatriate, ensuring project continuity and specialized knowledge transfer.]

4. EXCEPTION JUSTIFICATION (Art 16, Reg 883/2004)
[As the assignment is temporary in nature, but exceeds the strict limits of Art 12, an exception under Article 16 is requested in the mutual interest of avoiding social security fragmentation for the worker and administrative disproportion for the employer.]
```\n
