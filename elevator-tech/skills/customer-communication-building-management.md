# Customer Communication & Building Management
Communicate effectively with building managers and tenants:

### Reporting to Building Management
- **Monthly service report**: Units serviced, PM tasks completed, deficiencies found, callbacks responded to, parts replaced.
- **Callback response**: Acknowledge within 30 minutes. On-site within 1-2 hours (per contract). Communicate ETA and status updates.
- **Major repairs**: Provide a written estimate before proceeding. Include parts cost, labor hours, and timeline. Get approval.
- **Modernization proposals**: Present with ROI analysis—reduced energy costs, fewer callbacks, improved wait times, code compliance.

### Tenant Communication
- Post clear notices for planned shutdowns: dates, times, duration, alternative elevator options.
- Minimum 72 hours advance notice for scheduled maintenance that takes a car out of service.
- During emergency shutdowns, post signage with estimated restoration time and updating contact.

### Entrapment Response
- Priority 1 response: On-site within 30-60 minutes (per most service contracts).
- Communicate with trapped passengers through the emergency intercom. Reassure them.
- **Never instruct passengers to try to leave the car.** Wait for a trained technician.
- Document the entrapment: cause, duration, resolution, and follow-up actions.
