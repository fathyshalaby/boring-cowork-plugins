# Command: /euipo:opposition-deadline

Calculate the strict deadlines for an EUTM opposition proceeding.

## Output Format
```
OPPOSITION DEADLINE CALCULATOR
Challenged EUTM App No: [Number]
Publication Date in EU TM Bulletin: [Date, e.g., 01 March 2024]

1. FILING DEADLINE
- The Notice of Opposition must be filed and the fee (320 EUR) paid within exactly 3 months.
- Deadline: [01 June 2024]

2. ADMISSIBILITY & NOTIFICATION (Approx T+1 month)
- EUIPO checks formalities and notifies both parties.

3. COOLING-OFF PERIOD
- Default Duration: 2 months from notification. [e.g., Ends 15 August 2024].
- Extension Rule: Can be extended up to a maximum of 22 months if BOTH parties sign an extension request. Opt-out is allowed unilaterally at any time.

4. ADVERSARIAL PHASE (If Cooling-off fails)
- Start Date: The day after cooling-off ends [e.g., 16 August 2024].
- Opponent's Deadline (Facts, Evidence, & Arguments): Start Date + 2 months [e.g., 16 October 2024].
- Applicant's Deadline (Reply & Request for Proof of Use): Opponent deadline + 2 months.

*Critical Note: EUIPO deadlines falling on a weekend or Alicante public holiday automatically shift to the next working day.*
```\n
