# Command: /euipo:proof-of-use-index

Generate an organized index for filing Proof of Genuine Use evidence.

## Output Format
```
INDEX OF EVIDENCE: PROOF OF GENUINE USE
Mark: [Client Mark]
Relevant 5-Year Period: [e.g., 10 May 2019 to 09 May 2024]
Territory: [European Union]

ANNEX 1: FINANCIAL DATA (Place, Extent, Time)
- Annex 1A: Sworn Affidavit of CFO stating annual EU turnover for marked goods (Page 1-3).
- Annex 1B: Selection of 50 representative commercial invoices addressed to customers in Germany, France, and Italy, dated continuously between 2019-2024. Marker name highlighted in red. (Pages 4-54).

ANNEX 2: PRODUCT & PACKAGING (Nature of Use)
- Annex 2A: High-resolution photographs of the physical product packaging (Class 3 cosmetics) clearly showing the trademark affixed. (Pages 55-58).

ANNEX 3: MARKETING & ADVERTISING (Extent, Time)
- Annex 3A: Copies of multi-page print advertisements in "Vogue Europe" (Issues: June 2020, Dec 2022, Jan 2024). (Pages 59-65).
- Annex 3B: Invoices from media-buying agencies corroborating the ad spend for the above print ads. (Pages 66-70).
- Annex 3C: Google Analytics localized report showing 500k unique EU web visitors landing on the specific product page. (Pages 71-75).

CONCLUSION NOTE FOR EXAMINER
Evidence demonstrates continuous, real commercial exploitation in multiple Member States spanning the entire relevant period, explicitly for the registered goods in Class 3.
```\n
