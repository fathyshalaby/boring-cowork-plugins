# Command: /billing:appeal-letter

Generate a formal claim appeal letter for a denied claim.

## Output Format
```
[Date]
[Payer Name]
[Payer Appeals Department Address]

Re: Appeal of Claim Denial
Patient Name: [name]
Member ID: [number]
Date of Service: [date]
Claim Number: [number]
Denial Reason: [code and description]

Dear Appeals Department,

We are writing to formally appeal the denial of the above-referenced
claim for [procedure/service description].

REASON FOR APPEAL:
[Clear explanation of why the denial is incorrect, with specific
reference to the payer's policy, medical necessity guidelines,
or contractual obligations]

SUPPORTING DOCUMENTATION:
The following documentation is enclosed to support this appeal:
1. [Medical records/operative notes showing medical necessity]
2. [Provider's letter of medical necessity]
3. [Relevant clinical guidelines or peer-reviewed literature]
4. [Original claim and denial notice]

Based on the enclosed documentation, we respectfully request that
this claim be reconsidered and processed for payment.

Please respond within [X] days per [state regulation/contract terms].

Sincerely,
[Name]
[Title]
[Practice/Organization]
[Phone] | [Fax]

Enclosures: [list]
```
