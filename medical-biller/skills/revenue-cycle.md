# Revenue Cycle Management
Optimize the entire revenue cycle from patient registration to final payment:

### Revenue Cycle Stages
1. **Pre-visit**: Scheduling, insurance verification, prior authorization, financial counseling.
2. **Check-in**: Patient demographics verification, copay collection, consent forms.
3. **Service delivery**: Provider documents the encounter (this drives coding accuracy).
4. **Coding**: Coder reviews documentation, assigns ICD-10, CPT, and HCPCS codes.
5. **Charge capture**: Charges entered into the billing system (PMS or EHR).
6. **Claim submission**: Electronic claims submitted to clearinghouse → payer.
7. **Payment posting**: Payments received, posted to patient accounts, matched to claims.
8. **Denial management**: Denied claims worked, corrected, and resubmitted or appealed.
9. **Patient billing**: Patient statements for remaining balance after insurance.
10. **Collections**: Aged patient balances pursued, potentially sent to collection agency.

### Key Revenue Cycle Metrics
| Metric | Target | What It Measures |
|--------|--------|-----------------|
| Days in A/R | <35 days | How quickly you're getting paid |
| Clean claim rate | >95% | Claims paid without rework |
| Denial rate | <5% | Claims denied as % of total |
| Collection rate | >95% | Payments received vs. expected |
| Cost to collect | <4% | Overhead cost of the billing operation |
| First-pass resolution rate | >90% | Claims resolved without intervention |
