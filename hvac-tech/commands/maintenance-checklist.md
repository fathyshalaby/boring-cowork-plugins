# Command: /hvac:maintenance-checklist

Generate a comprehensive seasonal preventive maintenance checklist for a residential HVAC system.

## Usage
Specify:
- Season: **Cooling** (spring/summer) or **Heating** (fall/winter)
- System type: Central AC, Heat Pump, Gas Furnace, Boiler, or Mini-Split

## Cooling Season Checklist
```
SPRING/SUMMER PREVENTIVE MAINTENANCE CHECKLIST

OUTDOOR UNIT (CONDENSER):
☐ Inspect and clean condenser coil (wash with coil cleaner and garden hose)
☐ Straighten bent condenser fins with a fin comb
☐ Clear debris, vegetation, and obstructions (maintain 2ft clearance)
☐ Check refrigerant pressures, superheat, and subcooling
☐ Inspect contactor for pitting or arcing
☐ Test capacitor(s) with a multimeter (within 10% of rated μF)
☐ Measure compressor amp draw (compare to RLA on nameplate)
☐ Inspect electrical connections and tighten

INDOOR UNIT (AIR HANDLER/FURNACE):
☐ Replace or clean air filter
☐ Inspect evaporator coil (clean if accessible)
☐ Check condensate drain line (flush with vinegar or bleach solution)
☐ Test blower motor and measure amp draw
☐ Inspect blower wheel for dirt buildup
☐ Verify thermostat operation and calibration

SYSTEM PERFORMANCE:
☐ Measure supply and return air temperatures (delta-T: 14–22°F)
☐ Check airflow at registers
☐ Verify system cycles on and off normally
```
