# Command: /facility:vendor-scorecard

Generate a quarterly vendor performance evaluation scorecard.

## Output Format
```
VENDOR PERFORMANCE SCORECARD
Vendor: [name] | Service: [category]
Period: [Quarter/Year] | Evaluated By: [manager name]

SCORING CRITERIA (1-5 scale):
                                Score    Notes
────────────────────────────────────────────────
Responsiveness                  [X]/5    [comment]
Quality of Work                 [X]/5    [comment]
PM Schedule Adherence           [X]/5    [comment]
Communication                   [X]/5    [comment]
Invoice Accuracy                [X]/5    [comment]
Safety Compliance               [X]/5    [comment]

OVERALL SCORE: [X]/30 ([X]%)

INCIDENTS THIS PERIOD:
• [Any complaints, missed appointments, quality issues]

RECOMMENDATION:
☐ Continue contract as-is
☐ Schedule performance improvement meeting
☐ Issue formal warning
☐ Solicit replacement bids
```
