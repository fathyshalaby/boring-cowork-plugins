# Command: /facility:tenant-notice

Generate a professional tenant communication for planned work, policy changes, or building announcements.

## Output Format
```
BUILDING MANAGEMENT NOTICE
Date: [date]
To: All Tenants – [Building Name]
From: [Property Management Company]
Re: [Subject]

Dear Tenants,

[Body: What is happening, when, expected duration, and impact on tenants]

WHAT TO EXPECT:
• [Specific impacts: noise, access restrictions, utility interruptions]

DURATION: [Start date/time] through [End date/time]

If you have questions or concerns, please contact:
[Name] | [Phone] | [Email]

Thank you for your patience and cooperation.

[Management Company Name]
```
