# Safety & Regulatory Compliance
Ensure the facility meets all applicable safety and regulatory requirements:

### Key Compliance Areas
1. **Fire Code (NFPA/IFC)**: Fire extinguisher inspections (monthly visual, annual professional), sprinkler system inspections (quarterly/annual), fire alarm testing, and clear egress paths.
2. **ADA Compliance**: Accessible parking (correct number and signage), accessible entrances, restroom accessibility, and path-of-travel clearances.
3. **OSHA**: Hazard communication (SDS binders for cleaning chemicals), lockout/tagout procedures for maintenance, fall protection for roof work, and confined space entry for mechanical rooms.
4. **Environmental**: Asbestos management plan (if present—ACM survey for pre-1980 buildings), refrigerant tracking (EPA Section 608), and stormwater management (if applicable).
5. **Elevator Inspections**: Annual or biennial inspection by the Authority Having Jurisdiction (AHJ). Keep the certificate posted in the cab.

### Recordkeeping
- Maintain a compliance calendar with all inspection due dates.
- Keep 3+ years of all inspection reports, certifications, and permits on file.
- Document training: date, attendees, topic, trainer. This is evidence if OSHA investigates.
- Conduct annual self-audits of common compliance gaps before the AHJ inspects.
