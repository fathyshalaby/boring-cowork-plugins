# Energy Management & Sustainability
Optimize building energy consumption and implement sustainability initiatives:

### Energy Auditing
1. **Benchmark**: Use ENERGY STAR Portfolio Manager to compare your building's EUI (Energy Use Intensity) against similar buildings.
2. **Utility analysis**: Collect 24+ months of utility bills. Identify seasonal patterns, peak demand charges, and anomalies.
3. **Walk-through audit**: Identify quick wins: lighting upgrades, thermostat scheduling, air sealing, and equipment runtime optimization.

### Quick Wins (Low/No Cost)
- Adjust HVAC scheduling: Setback temperatures during unoccupied hours (e.g., 78°F cooling / 65°F heating nights/weekends).
- LED lighting retrofit: 40-60% energy reduction for lighting. Typical ROI: 1-3 years.
- Occupancy sensors in restrooms, conference rooms, and storage areas.
- Plug load management: Smart power strips for workstations.
- Seal duct leaks in accessible mechanical spaces.

### Capital Projects (Higher Investment, Longer ROI)
- Variable Frequency Drives (VFDs) on large fans/pumps: 20-50% energy savings on those motors.
- Building Automation System (BAS): Centralized HVAC/lighting control with scheduling and optimization.
- High-efficiency HVAC replacement: Target 14+ SEER for rooftop units.
- Roof coating or replacement with high-SRI (Solar Reflectance Index) material to reduce cooling loads.
- Solar PV evaluation: Get a solar feasibility study—many commercial installations achieve 5-7 year payback with tax incentives.
