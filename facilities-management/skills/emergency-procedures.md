# Emergency Procedures & Business Continuity
Maintain comprehensive emergency response plans for the facility:

### Emergency Types & Response Protocols
1. **Fire**: Activate alarm → Call 911 → Evacuate → Account for occupants at assembly point → Do NOT re-enter.
2. **Severe Weather**: Monitor NWS alerts → Activate shelter-in-place protocol → Direct occupants to designated safe areas (interior, lowest floor, away from windows).
3. **Power Outage**: Verify generator auto-start → Check life safety systems (emergency lights, fire panel, elevators) → Communicate status to tenants → Notify utility.
4. **Water Leak/Flood**: Identify source → Shut off water (know your valve locations!) → Protect equipment and inventory → Call restoration company within 1 hour.
5. **Active Threat**: Run-Hide-Fight protocol. Coordinate with law enforcement. Do NOT use fire alarm for evacuation.

### Critical Documentation (Keep Updated & Accessible)
- Emergency contact list (property management, owner, fire department, police, utility companies, insurance, key vendors).
- Building floor plans with utility shutoff locations, fire exits, AED locations, and storm shelter areas.
- Tenant emergency contact list.
- Insurance policy summary and claim filing procedure.
- Building-specific hazmat information (SDS sheets for chemicals on-site).

### Drills & Training
- Fire drill: Minimum annually (semi-annually recommended). Time the evacuation and identify bottlenecks.
- Severe weather drill: Annually before storm season.
- AED/CPR: At least 2 trained staff per shift.
- Document ALL drills: date, participants, time to evacuate, observations, and corrective actions.
