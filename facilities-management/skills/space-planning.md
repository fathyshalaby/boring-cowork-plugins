# Space Planning & Tenant Coordination
Manage space allocation, moves, and tenant improvements efficiently:

### Space Planning Fundamentals
- **Usable vs. Rentable**: Rentable SF includes the tenant's pro-rata share of common areas (lobbies, restrooms, corridors). The load factor is typically 10-20%.
- **Space standards**: Open office: 100-150 SF/person. Private office: 150-250 SF. Conference room: 25 SF/person.
- **Occupancy codes**: Know the maximum occupancy per floor based on fire code (IBC Table 1004.5). Don't exceed code limits.

### Move Management
1. **Planning**: Create a detailed move plan with timeline, vendor assignments, and IT coordination.
2. **Furniture**: Coordinate with furniture vendor for disassembly, transport, and reassembly.
3. **IT/Telecom**: Network drops, phone assignments, and printer relocations. Coordinate with IT department 2-4 weeks ahead.
4. **Signage**: Update directories, wayfinding, and nameplate signage.
5. **Communication**: Notify security, reception, mail/package delivery, janitorial, and parking management.
6. **Post-move punch list**: Walk the space with the occupant within 48 hours to capture any issues.

### Tenant Improvement (TI) Projects
- Develop a clear scope of work before engaging contractors.
- Get 3 competitive bids for any TI project over $10,000.
- Verify contractor licenses, insurance, and bonding.
- Require daily cleanup and coordinate work hours to minimize tenant disruption.
- Inspect work at rough-in and at completion before releasing final payment.
