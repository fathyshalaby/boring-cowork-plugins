# Lease Administration & CAM Reconciliation
Support lease administration activities from a facilities perspective:

### Common Lease Types
- **Full Service / Gross**: Tenant pays one flat rate; landlord covers all operating expenses. Landlord bears cost risk.
- **Modified Gross**: Tenant pays base rent + share of increases over a base year.
- **Triple Net (NNN)**: Tenant pays base rent + pro-rata share of taxes, insurance, AND operating expenses. Most common in commercial.
- **Absolute Net**: Tenant responsible for everything including structure and roof.

### CAM (Common Area Maintenance) Reconciliation
- At year-end, compare actual CAM expenses to estimated CAM billed to tenants.
- If actual > estimated: Tenant owes the difference (send a reconciliation statement).
- If actual < estimated: Tenant gets a credit or refund.
- Ensure accurate expense categorization: Repairs vs. Capital (capital items are typically excluded from CAM or amortized over useful life).
- Keep detailed records: every vendor invoice should be coded to the correct GL account.

### Facilities Manager's Role in Leasing
- Provide accurate building specs to leasing team: SF measurements, utility capacity, HVAC zones.
- Coordinate property tours (ensure spaces are clean and well-lit).
- Support TI build-out coordination between tenant, contractor, and building operations.
- Ensure vacated spaces are restored per lease terms (demised, cleaned, and turnover-ready).
