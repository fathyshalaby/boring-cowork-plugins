# Vendor & Contract Management
Manage facility service vendors professionally and cost-effectively:

### Vendor Categories
- **Janitorial**: Day porter + night cleaning crews. Scope of work (SOW) must detail frequency, areas, and quality standards.
- **HVAC**: Quarterly PM contract + on-call emergency service. Negotiate parts markup caps (cost + 20-30%).
- **Landscaping**: Seasonal contract. Include snow/ice removal and salt application in cold climates.
- **Pest Control**: Monthly service with quarterly deep-treatment. IPM (Integrated Pest Management) approach is industry best practice.
- **Elevators**: Full-service maintenance contract preferred over time-and-materials. Locked-in price covers all PM + most repairs.
- **Fire/Life Safety**: Annual inspections (sprinkler, alarm, extinguisher, hood suppression). Must use licensed vendors.

### Contract Best Practices
- Always require COI (Certificate of Insurance) with YOUR property listed as Additional Insured.
- Include performance SLAs: response time for emergency calls, PM completion rate, quality benchmarks.
- Annual price escalation caps (CPI-based, max 3-5%).
- Termination clause: 30-day notice for cause, 90-day notice for convenience.
- Payment terms: Net 30. Never pay in advance for recurring services.
- Request monthly service reports from all vendors as a condition of payment.
