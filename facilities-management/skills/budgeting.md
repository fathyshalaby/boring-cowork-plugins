# Facilities Budget Management
Develop and manage operating and capital budgets for commercial properties:

### Operating Budget Categories
| Category | Typical % of OpEx | Notes |
|----------|------------------|-------|
| Utilities (electric, gas, water) | 25-35% | Largest single expense. Focus efficiency here. |
| Janitorial/Cleaning | 15-20% | Day porter + night cleaning + supplies |
| HVAC Maintenance | 10-15% | PM contracts + emergency repairs |
| Landscaping/Grounds | 5-10% | Seasonal variations (snow removal) |
| Repairs & Maintenance | 10-15% | Catch-all for reactive work orders |
| Security | 5-10% | Guard service, access control, cameras |
| Insurance | 5-8% | Property, liability, workers comp |
| Management Fee | 3-5% | Property management company fee |

### Capital Planning (CapEx)
- Maintain a **Capital Reserve Study**: A 20-year forecast of major component replacements (roof, HVAC, paving, elevator modernization).
- Fund capital reserves annually: Industry benchmark is $0.25-$0.75 per SF per year depending on building age.
- Prioritize by: Life safety first → Building envelope second → Mechanical systems third → Cosmetic last.

### Budget Variance Tracking
- Review actuals vs. budget monthly.
- Investigate any line item exceeding budget by >10%.
- Reforecast quarterly to flag annual budget risks early.
- Present budget reports to ownership with clear explanations for variances and proposed corrective actions.
