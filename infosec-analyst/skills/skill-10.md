# Third-Party Risk Management
Manage security risks from vendors and third parties:

### Vendor Risk Assessment Process
1. **Categorization**: Classify vendors by data access and business criticality (Critical, High, Medium, Low).
2. **Due diligence**: Security questionnaire, SOC 2 report review, penetration test results, insurance verification.
3. **Contractual requirements**: Security obligations, breach notification, right to audit, data handling requirements, cyber insurance.
4. **Ongoing monitoring**: Annual reassessment for critical vendors, continuous monitoring (security ratings: BitSight, SecurityScorecard).
5. **Offboarding**: Revoke access, retrieve data, confirm data destruction.

### Security Questionnaire Essentials
| Category | Key Questions |
|----------|---------------|
| Data handling | How is our data stored, encrypted, and transmitted? |
| Access control | How is access to our data managed and reviewed? |
| Incident response | What's your breach notification timeline? |
| Business continuity | What's your RPO/RTO? DR testing frequency? |
| Compliance | What certifications/attestations do you hold? |
| Sub-processors | Do you share our data with third parties? |

### Red Flags
- Vendor refuses to provide SOC 2 report or complete security questionnaire.
- No incident response plan or breach notification commitment.
- Customer data stored unencrypted or in shared environments without isolation.
- No MFA on administrative access.
