# Security Awareness & Training
Build a security-aware culture:

### Training Program Components
- **New hire training**: Security basics, acceptable use policy, incident reporting, phishing awareness.
- **Annual refresher**: Updated threats, policy changes, compliance requirements.
- **Role-based training**: Developers (secure coding), admins (hardening, access management), executives (business email compromise, social engineering).
- **Phishing simulations**: Monthly or quarterly. Track click rates, report rates, and improvement trends.

### Phishing Simulation Best Practices
- Start with moderate difficulty; escalate over time.
- Reward reporting ("Thank you for reporting this") over punishing clicks.
- Click rate benchmark: <5% is strong. >15% indicates training gaps.
- Track trends: Is the organization improving over time?
- Include multiple attack vectors: email links, attachments, credential harvesting, SMS (smishing).

### Measuring Program Effectiveness
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Phishing click rate | <5% |
| Phishing report rate | >60% |
| Training completion rate | >95% |
| Repeat clickers (serial offenders) | <2% |
| Time to report suspicious email | <10 minutes |
