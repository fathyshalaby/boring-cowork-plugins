# Compliance & Audit Management
Manage security compliance programs and audit processes:

### Common Compliance Requirements
| Regulation | Applies To | Key Requirements |
|-----------|-----------|------------------|
| SOC 2 | Service organizations | Trust Service Criteria, annual audit |
| PCI DSS | Card data processors | 12 requirements, annual assessment |
| HIPAA | Healthcare / PHI | Security Rule (administrative, physical, technical safeguards) |
| GDPR | EU personal data | Privacy by design, DPO, breach notification 72 hrs |
| SOX (IT controls) | Public companies | IT general controls, access management, change management |
| CCPA/CPRA | California consumer data | Consumer rights, data inventory, opt-out |

### Audit Preparation
1. Maintain a **controls matrix**: Map each control to the applicable framework requirement(s).
2. Gather **evidence** continuously, not just before the audit. Use a GRC platform (Vanta, Drata, Tugboat Logic).
3. Conduct **internal audits** or readiness assessments before the external audit.
4. Assign **control owners**: Every control has a named individual responsible for its operation and evidence.
5. **Gap remediation**: Address gaps before the audit period, not during.

### Evidence Types
- Policies and procedures (documented and current)
- System configurations and screenshots
- Access review records
- Change management logs
- Training records
- Vulnerability scan reports
- Incident response documentation
