# Command: /dpo:lia-template

Generate an LIA structure for relying on Article 6(1)(f).

## Output Format
```
LEGITIMATE INTERESTS ASSESSMENT (LIA)
Processing Activity: [Name/Description]
Controller: [Organization]
Date Completed: [Date]

PART 1: THE PURPOSE TEST
What is the legitimate interest behind the processing?
[Describe the benefit to the organization, third parties, or wider society. E.g., direct marketing, fraud prevention, physical security.]
Is it lawful, ethical, and clearly articulated?

PART 2: THE NECESSITY TEST
Is the processing necessary to achieve the objective?
[Can the goal be achieved by a less intrusive means? Are alternative lawful bases unviable? Why is this specific data needed?]

PART 3: THE BALANCING TEST
Nature of the data: [Is it sensitive? Special category? Children's data?]
Reasonable expectations: [Would the individual expect you to use their data this way?]
Likely impact: [What is the risk of harm, distress, or financial loss?]
Safeguards in place: [Opt-out options, encryption, short retention periods]

CONCLUSION
Do the legitimate interests override the individual's rights and freedoms?
☐ YES (Proceed relying on Legitimate Interests)
☐ NO (Find a different lawful basis or cease processing)

Approved by: [DPO Name]
```
