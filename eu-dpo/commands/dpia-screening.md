# Command: /dpo:dpia-screening

Generate a preliminary assessment to determine if a full DPIA is required.

## Output Format
```
DPIA SCREENING QUESTIONNAIRE
Project/System: [Name]
Business Owner: [Name/Department]

Does the processing involve any of the following? (Check all that apply)
☐ Evaluation or scoring (including profiling or predicting behavior)
☐ Automated decision-making with legal or similar significant effect
☐ Systematic monitoring (e.g., tracking employee location or internet usage)
☐ Sensitive data or data of a highly personal nature (Art. 9 or criminal data)
☐ Data processed on a large scale
☐ Matching or combining datasets from different processing operations
☐ Data concerning vulnerable data subjects (e.g., children, employees)
☐ Innovative use or applying new technological/organizational solutions
☐ Processing that prevents subjects from exercising a right or using a service

DPO PRELIMINARY ASSESSMENT
Requires Full DPIA? ☐ YES  ☐ NO

Rationale:
[Explain why the risk threshold is or isn't met based on WP29 guidelines]

Next Steps:
[Instructions for the business owner]
```
