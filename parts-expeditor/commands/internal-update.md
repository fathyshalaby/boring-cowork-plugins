# Command: /parts:internal-update

Generate an internal stakeholder update on material availability.

## Output Format
```
MATERIAL STATUS UPDATE
Date: [date] | From: Parts Expediting
To: [Production/Engineering/Management]

SUMMARY: [1-2 sentence overview]

ON-TIME DELIVERY PERFORMANCE: [X]% (target: 95%)

CRITICAL ITEMS:
Part         PO#     Impact              Recovery Plan        ETA
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
[P/N]       [PO]    [what's at risk]     [what we're doing]   [date]

COMMANDEERED/PREMIUM FREIGHT:
PO#        Cost      Justification
────────────────────────────────────
[PO]       $[X]      [reason]

UPCOMING RISKS:
[Items that may become issues in next 2-4 weeks]

ACTIONS NEEDED FROM YOU:
[Any decisions or approvals required]
```
