# BOM Management & Analysis
Manage Bills of Materials for accurate ordering:

### BOM Fundamentals
- **BOM (Bill of Materials)**: Structured list of all parts required to build or maintain an assembly.
- **BOM levels**: Top-level assembly → sub-assemblies → individual components → raw materials.
- **BOM accuracy target**: ≥98%. Inaccurate BOMs cause wrong parts, wrong quantities, and production delays.

### BOM Analysis for Procurement
1. **Explode the BOM**: Calculate total part requirements for the production schedule.
2. **Net against inventory**: On-hand + On-order - Allocated = Available. Order the shortfall.
3. **Lead time analysis**: Identify long-lead items early. These drive the procurement timeline.
4. **Single-source risk**: Flag BOM items with only one qualified supplier.

### Engineering Change Management
- When engineering releases a revision (ECO/ECN), update BOMs immediately.
- Disposition existing stock: Can old-revision parts be used? Consumed? Returned? Scrapped?
- Communicate changes to suppliers before the next order.
- Update purchase orders to reflect the new revision level.
