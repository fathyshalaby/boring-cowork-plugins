# Supplier Performance Management
Track and improve supplier performance:

### Supplier Scorecard Metrics
| Metric | Calculation | Target |
|--------|------------|--------|
| On-Time Delivery (OTD) | (On-time deliveries ÷ Total deliveries) × 100% | ≥95% |
| Quality Rejection Rate | (Rejected lots ÷ Total lots received) × 100% | <2% |
| Lead Time Accuracy | (Actual lead time ÷ Quoted lead time) × 100% | ≤105% |
| Responsiveness | Average time to respond to RFQs and inquiries | <24 hours |
| Documentation Accuracy | (Error-free documents ÷ Total shipments) × 100% | ≥98% |

### Quarterly Business Reviews
1. Review scorecard metrics with the supplier.
2. Discuss: On-time delivery trends, quality issues and root causes, upcoming demand forecast, capacity concerns, cost reduction opportunities.
3. Agree on improvement actions with deadlines.
4. Document meeting notes and distribute.

### Corrective Action Process
- Issue a Supplier Corrective Action Request (SCAR) for significant quality or delivery failures.
- Require root cause analysis and corrective/preventive action plan from the supplier.
- Verify effectiveness at subsequent deliveries.
- Persistent poor performers: reduce volume, qualify alternate sources, consider removal from AVL.
