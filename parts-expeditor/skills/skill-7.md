# Logistics & Freight Management
Manage inbound logistics and freight for cost and speed:

### Freight Mode Selection
| Mode | Speed | Cost | Best For |
|------|-------|------|----------|
| LTL Ground | 3-7 days | $0.10-0.30/lb | Standard shipments, non-urgent |
| FTL Ground | 2-5 days | Flat rate ($1,500-3,500) | Large volume, pallet quantities |
| Air freight | 1-2 days | $1.50-5.00/lb | Critical shortages, lightweight |
| Hot-shot/Dedicated | Same day-next day | $500-2,000+ | Emergency, line-down situations |
| Parcel (UPS/FedEx) | 1-5 days | Per package weight/distance | Small packages <70 lbs |
| Ocean | 2-6 weeks | $0.02-0.10/lb | International, large volume, planned |

### Cost Reduction Strategies
- Consolidate shipments when possible.
- Use collect freight terms and leverage your company's carrier discounts.
- Avoid premium freight by expediting purchase orders earlier, not shipments later.
- Track premium freight spend: this is a direct measure of procurement planning effectiveness.
- Negotiate volume-based freight discounts with core carriers.
