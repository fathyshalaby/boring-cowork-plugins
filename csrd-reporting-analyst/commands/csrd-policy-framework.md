# Command: /csrd:csrd-policy-framework

Generate the required disclosures for describing a sustainability policy (ESRS 2 MDR-P).

## Output Format
```
POLICY DISCLOSURE VALIDATION
Policy Name: [e.g., Supplier Code of Conduct / Anti-Bribery Policy]
Target ESRS Topic: [e.g., G1 Business Conduct]

To comply with ESRS 2 MDR-P (Minimum Disclosure Requirements - Policies), the published policy description in the Management Report must include:

☐ 1. Summary of objectives: [What does the policy aim to achieve concerning the material matter?]
☐ 2. Scope of application: [Does it apply globally? Only EU? Does it extend to the upstream value chain (suppliers)?]
☐ 3. Top Management allocation: [Which C-level executive or Board Committee is ultimately accountable for this policy?]
☐ 4. Third-party standards/initiatives: [e.g., "This policy aligns with the UN Global Compact principles."]
☐ 5. Implementation Communication: [How is this made available to stakeholders? Internal intranet? Public website? Attached to supplier contracts?]

STAKEHOLDER ENGAGEMENT:
☐ 6. Was the policy developed in consultation with affected stakeholders? [e.g., Works Council consulted on the health & safety policy?]

REMEDIATION & GRIEVANCE:
☐ 7. Does the policy reference a grievance mechanism for stakeholders to report violations? [e.g., link to anonymous whistleblower hotline].

*If the company does NOT have a policy on a material sustainability matter, it MUST disclose this fact and state whether it plans to draft one and the timeframe for doing so (ESRS 2 - MDR-P paragraph 64).*
```
