# Command: /hr-wc:consultation-timeline

Generate a project management timeline for a complex Works Council consultation.

## Output Format
```
CONSULTATION TIMELINE STRATEGY
Project: [Name, e.g., Closing the Lyon Facility]
Legislation applicable: [French Labour Code, CSE consultation]

PRE-CONSULTATION PHASE
- Week 1: Finalize Business Case & Financial rationale (Management).
- Week 2: Draft 'Livre II' (Economic reasons) and 'Livre I' (Social plan/PSE).
- Week 3: Legal review of notification documents.
- Week 4: Informal confidential heads-up to CSE Secretary/Union Delegates.

KICK-OFF PHASE (Triggering the legal clock)
- Day 0: Send convocation and formal Info-Memo (Livre I & II) to CSE.
- Day 3 (Meeting 1): Presentation of the project. CSE decides on appointing an external expert.
- Day 5: Notification to Labour Authority (DIRECCTE/DREETS) initiating the process.

EXPERT REVIEW & NEGOTIATION PHASE
- Day 20 (Meeting 2): Q&A on the business case.
- Day 45: Expert submits their financial/social report to the CSE.
- Day 50 (Meeting 3): Discussion of Expert findings. Negotiation of the Social Plan (severance, training).

CLOSURE PHASE
- Day 60 (Final Meeting): Formal rendering of the CSE Opinion (Avis). It may be negative, but the procedure is fulfilled.
- Day 61: Submission of final documents and CSE opinion to Labour Authority for validation/homologation (Usually max 15-21 days for authority review).
- Day 80: Approval received. Implement dismissals.
```
