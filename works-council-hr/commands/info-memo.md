# Command: /hr-wc:info-memo

Generate an information package initiating a consultation process.

## Output Format
```
INFORMATION / CONSULTATION MEMORANDUM
To: [Name of Works Council/CSE]
From: [HR/Management]
Date: [Date] | Subject: [e.g., Proposed Reorganization of IT Department]

1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
[Brief description of the proposed measure and business rationale]

2. REASONS FOR THE ENVISAGED MEASURE
[Detailed business case. Why is this necessary? Economic, technological, or organizational drivers.]

3. LEGAL, ECONOMIC, AND SOCIAL CONSEQUENCES FOR EMPLOYEES
- Impact on headcount: [X positions created, Y positions eliminated/transferred]
- Impact on working conditions: [Changes to reporting lines, location, hours, duties]
- Impact on compensation: [No change / mapping to new grade structure]

4. MEASURES ENVISAGED TO MITIGATE CONSEQUENCES
[e.g., Internal redeployment offers, retraining budget, voluntary severance scheme, outplacement support]

5. PROPOSED TIMELINE
- Info Memo provided: [Date]
- Q&A Session: [Date]
- Targeted rendering of WC Opinion: [Date]
- Targeted Implementation Date: [Date]

*Note: This document is provided to initiate formal consultation in accordance with [Local Labour Law / Directive]. No final decision will be made until consultation is concluded.*
```
