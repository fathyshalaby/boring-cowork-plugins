# Command: /hr-wc:redundancy-criteria

Draft a proposal for Social Selection Criteria (Sozialauswahl) for redundancies.

## Output Format
```
PROPOSED SOCIAL SELECTION CRITERIA (Redundancy Scoring Matrix)
Business Unit: [Name]
Total roles to reduce within pool: [X]

To ensure fair and objective selection for collective redundancies within interchangeable job profiles, the following point system is proposed for Works Council consultation:

CRITERIA 1: LENGTH OF SERVICE (Seniority)
- 1 point per full year of service (up to 10 years).
- 2 points per full year of service (from 11-25 years).
(Maximum cap: [X] points)

CRITERIA 2: AGE
- 1 point per year of age above [e.g., 35 years].
(Maximum cap: [X] points)
*Note: High protection usually given to older workers closer to retirement.*

CRITERIA 3: MAINTENANCE OBLIGATIONS (Dependents)
- [X] points for a dependent spouse.
- [X] points per dependent child.

CRITERIA 4: SEVERE DISABILITY
- [X] points for recognized severe disability status.

EXCEPTIONS (Top Performers/Key Knowledge Workers)
Management reserves the right to exclude up to [e.g., 10%] of the pool from the redundancy selection based on justified operational needs (specialized skills, extraordinary performance), subject to review by the Works Council.

SELECTION MECHANISM:
Employees with the LOWEST total points within the interchangeable pool will be selected for redundancy.
```
