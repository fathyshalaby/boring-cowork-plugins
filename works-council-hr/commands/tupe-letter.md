# Command: /hr-wc:tupe-letter

Draft the mandatory information letter regarding a business transfer.

## Output Format
```
NOTICE OF TRANSFER OF EMPLOYMENT (TUPE/Acquired Rights Directive)
Date: [Date]
To: [Employee Name]

Dear [Name],

As you may be aware, [Current Company/Transferor] has agreed to sell its [Business Unit Name] business to [New Company/Transferee].

1. FACT AND DATE OF TRANSFER
This transaction will constitute a transfer of an undertaking under [Local TUPE Law equivalent]. Your employment will transfer automatically to [New Company] on [Completion Date, e.g., midnight on 31 December 2024].

2. LEGAL, ECONOMIC, AND SOCIAL IMPLICATIONS FOR YOU
Your employment contract will transfer intact. Your continuity of service is preserved. Your current base salary, working hours, and notice period remain unchanged.
[If applying: Mention changes to pensions if local law allows, or changes to collective agreements].

3. MEASURES ENVISAGED
[New Company] plans to [e.g., harmonize payroll dates, relocate the office 5 miles away, change the healthcare provider to a comparable plan].
OR: [New Company] does not envisage taking any measures in connection with the transfer that will materially affect your working conditions.

4. RIGHT TO OBJECT
Under local law, you have the right to object to the transfer of your employment. If you object, your employment will definitively end on the transfer date, and this will be treated as a resignation, not a dismissal. Please notify HR by [Date] if you intend to object.

Sincerely,
[HR Director, Transferor]  &  [HR Director, Transferee]
```
